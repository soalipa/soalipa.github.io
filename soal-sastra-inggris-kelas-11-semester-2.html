3. sastra Inggris kelas XI semester 1​


Jawaban:

Kalimat ke-1

Conditional type 2: I would take you a holiday if I had moneyIn fact: I will not take you a holiday because I don't have money

Kalimat ke-2

Conditional type 2: I would love you if you took me to flyIn fact: I will not love you because you don't take me to fly

Kalimat ke-3

Conditional type 2: I would run away if you tried to put me in a big troubleIn fact: I will not run away because you don't put me in big trouble

Kalimat ke-4

Conditional type 2: I would be smart if you taught meIn fact: I will not be smart because you don't teach me

Kalimat ke-5

Conditional type 2: I would teach you how to learn English if my mother allowed meIn fact: I will not teach you how to learn English because my mother doesn't allow me

Penjelasan:

Kalimat conditional berupa kalimat yang mencirikan sesuatu yang tidak berdasarkan fakta, melainkan fakta berbeda dengan kondisi yang ada.

Penggunaan:

S + would + V1 + ROS + if + O + V2 + ROSIn fact + S + will + not + V1 + ROS + because + O + don't/doesn't + V1 + ROS

ROS = rest of sentence (O + C)

Contohnya:

I would go if you accompanied me, in fact I will not go because you don't accompany me

I would go hanya suatu kata yang diharapkan agar semoga obyek dapat menemani subyek pergi tapi pada kenyataan subyek tidak akan pergi karena obyek tidak menemaninya

Kesimpulan:

Conditional sentence merupakan kalimat yang mencirikan seseorang terhadap sesuatu yang bukan berdasarkan fakta. Sesuatu yang diambakan harap dapat terjadi pada kenyataan. Tapi, itu semua berdasarkan kondisi/keadaan yang ada dimana subyek belum tentu bisa mendapatkan/melakukan apa yang ada di pikirannya

Details

Mata pelajaran: Bahasa Inggris

Materi: Conditional Sentence

Tingkatan: Sekolah Menengah Atas

Kata kunci: Conditional Sentence Type 2

6. sastra Inggris halaman 91 kelas 10​


Jawaban

Thatcher was born in Grantham, Lincolnshire, on October 13 1925. Her father was Alfred Roberts and her mother was Beatrice Ethel. When she was a child, they lived in Grantham, where her father ran two grocery shops. Her parents raised her and her older sister, Muriel, in the flat above the grocery shop on North Parade near the railway line. Her father was an active member of Liberal party. He brought up his daughters strictly. He was appointed the Mayor of Grantham in 1945-1946. When the Labour party won (teks terpotong) he lost his position as alderman.

Penjelasan

Teks di atas merupakan biography (biografi) dari seseorang bernama Thatcher.

Ciri khas dari teks biografi adalah menggunakan simple past tense dikarenakan menceritakan sesuatu yang terjadi di masa lampau.

Simple past tense menggunakan kata kerja bentuk kedua (Verb 2)

Maka kita bisa melengkapi teks tersebut menggunakan kata kerja bentuk kedua (V2) sebagai berikut:

Was (subjek singular, Thatcher)Was (subjek singular, her father)Was (subjek singular, her mother)Was (subjek singular, she)Live - Lived - Lived (regular verb)Run - Ran - Run (irregular verb)Raise - Raised - Raised (regular verb)Was (subjek singular, her father)Bring up - Brought up - Brought up (irregular verb)Was (subjek singular, he)Win - Won - Won (irregular verb)Lose - Lost - Lost (irregular verb)

Catatan!

To be past = Was/were (was untuk subjek singular, were untuk subjek plural)Regular Verb = Kata kerja lampau berakhiran -edIrregular Verb = Kata kerja lampau tidak berakhiran -ed

Make conditional sentences type 1 using the verbs in the brackets.

1. If Dony studies hard, he will get good scores.

2. We will take a trip to America if we have some money.

3. If Maya prepares her homework every night, she will get better grades.

4. If Rina has enough money, she will treat my friend.

5. He will answer all your questions if he is here.

6. If she has some reference books, she will finish her homework faster.

7. If he is Albert Einstein, he will be very famous.

Rumus conditional sentence type 1 adalah

If + subject + verb 1, subject + will + verb 1.

Id + simple present tense, subject + simple future tense.

25. sastra inggris halaman 172 activity 8 kelas 10​


Jawaban:

Activity 8 on page 172 of the English Literature textbook for grade 10 asks students to analyze the use of imagery in the poem "The Raven" by Edgar Allan Poe. Students are asked to identify the different types of imagery used in the poem, and to explain how they contribute to the overall meaning of the poem.

Here are some of the different types of imagery used in "The Raven":

* Visual imagery: This type of imagery uses words to create pictures in the reader's mind. For example, the line "Once upon a midnight dreary, while I pondered, weak and weary" uses visual imagery to create a picture of a dark and gloomy night.

* Auditory imagery: This type of imagery uses words to create sounds in the reader's mind. For example, the line "And the silken sad uncertain rustling of each purple curtain thrilled me" uses auditory imagery to create the sound of curtains rustling in the wind.

* Olfactory imagery: This type of imagery uses words to create smells in the reader's mind. For example, the line "And the silken, sad, uncertain rustling of each purple curtain thrilled me" uses olfactory imagery to create the smell of dust and decay.
