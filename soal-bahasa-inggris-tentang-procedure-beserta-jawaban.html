21. The text above shows us how to …

Title => Tujuan dari tutorial, misalnya “How to Make a Pancake”Materials/Ingredients => Bahan-bahan yang dibutuhkanSteps => Langkah-langkah cara membuat/melakukannya

PEMBAHASAN

================

Untuk melengkapi kalimat di atas jawaban yang tepat adalah...

(A) will get

Alasannya adalah karena kalimat di atas bentuk CS Type 1 dimana setelah "if you do" disini kata kerjnya menggunakan V1, So conditional nya harus will + V1 "will get"


Karena rumus conditional type 1

If + simple present, conditional (will+V1)

Kalimat ini adalah contoh kalimat First Conditional/Conditional Sentence Type 1, diketahui dari tense yang digunakan setelah kata "if", yaitu Simple Present Tense (you do this test well).

Maka klausa setelahnya(setelah tanda koma), menggunakan Simple Future Tense. (you will get a good mark).

Rumus First Conditional: If + (simple present tense), (simple future tense).

JAWABANNYA: A

How to Make Brownies 


Ingredients
• 150gram of butter.
• 150gram of dark chocolate.
• 300 gram of flour                 
• 4 eggs.
• 600 gram of regular sugar
• 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps
1. Melt the chocolate with the butter.
2. Stir the eggs with the sugar and the vanilla extract.
3. Preheat the oven at low temperature
4. Combine the mixes you made.
5. Add the flour and stir with a wooden spoon.
6. Grease a brownie tin.
7. Add a little flour to cover the tin.
8. Add the brownie dough.
9. Bake for 10-30 minutes.
10. To check the brownies, put a knife in the mix. The knife must be moist!
11. Eat them 15 minutes after you took them out of the oven.

Tentu, berikut adalah teks prosedur dalam bahasa Inggris tentang cara membuat secangkir teh, diikuti dengan 10 soal dan jawaban:

**Procedure: How to Make a Cup of Tea**

*Introduction:*
Making a cup of tea is a simple and comforting process. Follow these steps to brew a perfect cup of tea.

**Materials Needed:**
1. A tea bag or loose tea leaves
2. A cup or mug
3. Boiling water
4. A teaspoon
5. A saucer (optional)
6. Sugar, honey, or milk (optional)

**Steps:**
1. Boil Water:
- Start by boiling water in a kettle. Make sure it's fresh and cold.

2. Preheat the Cup:
- While the water is boiling, pour a small amount of hot water into your cup to preheat it. Swirl it around and then discard the water.

3. Add the Tea Bag or Tea Leaves:
- If using a tea bag, place it into the cup. If using loose tea leaves, add one teaspoon of tea leaves to the cup.

4. Pour Hot Water:
- Once the water reaches a rolling boil, pour it into the cup over the tea bag or tea leaves.

5. Steep the Tea:
- Let the tea steep for 3-5 minutes. The longer you steep, the stronger the flavor.

6. Remove the Tea Bag or Strain the Tea:
- If you used a tea bag, remove it from the cup. If you used loose tea leaves, strain the tea to remove them.

7. Customize Your Tea (Optional):
- Add sugar, honey, or milk to your tea according to your preference. Stir well.

8. Serve:
- Place a saucer under the cup (if you like), and serve your tea hot.

9. Enjoy:
- Find a cozy spot, sip your tea, and savor the soothing flavor.

**10 Questions and Answers:**

1. What is the first step in making tea?
- Boiling water.

2. Why should you preheat the cup?
- To maintain the tea's temperature.

3. How long should you let the tea steep?
- 3-5 minutes.

4. What should you do after steeping the tea?
- Remove the tea bag or strain the tea.

5. What can you add to customize your tea?
- Sugar, honey, or milk.

6. What do you do if you're using loose tea leaves?
- Add one teaspoon of tea leaves to the cup.

7. What is the purpose of a saucer?
- It's optional but can be used to place the cup on.

8. When should you pour the boiling water over the tea bag or tea leaves?
- After it reaches a rolling boil.

9. What should you do before discarding the hot water used for preheating the cup?
- Swirl it around in the cup.

10. How do you enjoy your cup of tea?
- Find a cozy spot and savor the soothing flavor.

                                      How To Make Mango juice

Materials: 2 mangoes, sugar, sweetened condensed milk, water, and ice.

Steps:

Peel the mango and wash it with clean water. Cut the mango flesh according to taste.If the mango has been cut into small pieces, puree in a blender with water.Add sugar, sweetened condensed milk and a little ice to the blender.Puree until the mango flesh is really smooth.When the mango is smooth, strain the mango and serve in a serving glass.Add ice cubes to the glass and add milk on top.Mango juice is ready to serve.

Artinya yaitu:

                             Cara Membuat Jus Mangga

Bahan: 2 buah mangga, gula pasir, susu kental manis, air, dan es batu.

Langkah:

Kupas mangga dan cuci dengan air bersih. Potong daging mangga sesuai selera. Jika mangga sudah dipotong kecil-kecil, haluskan dengan blender bersama air. Tambahkan gula pasir, susu kental manis dan sedikit es batu ke dalam blender. Haluskan hingga daging mangga benar-benar halus. Mangga yang sudah halus, saring mangga dan sajikan dalam gelas saji.Tambahkan es batu ke dalam gelas dan tambahkan susu di atasnya.Jus mangga siap disajikan.

how to make apple pie:

In a small bowl, combine sugars, flour and spices. In a large bowl, toss apples with lemon juice. Add sugar mixture; toss to coat. Add filling; dot with butter.

36. persamaan dan perbedaan procedure text dan manual procedure (text dalam bahasa Inggris) ​


Jawaban:

helloooo saya bantu jawab yah :)

PERSAMAAN (equality/sameness)

1. General Purpose (tujuan umum): Both procedure text and manual procedure have the general purpose of providing instructions or guidance on how to perform a specific action or process.

2. Similar Structure (struktur serupa): They both have a similar structure, consisting of steps or a sequence of actions that must be followed by the reader or user.

DIFFERENCE (perbedaan)

1. Content and Expertise (konten dan keahlian): Procedure Text is for everyday tasks like cooking or personal care and is easy to understand. Manual Procedure is for technical or professional tasks, often requiring specialized knowledge.

2. Context (konteks): Procedure Text is used in everyday situations, while Manual Procedure is used in technical or industrial settings where precision and safety are critical.

How to Make Avocado Juice

Ingredients:

1 avocado

1 cup water/milk

1.5 tsp brown sugar

5 tbsp milk power

*omit milk powder if u r using milk

How to make:

Cut in between the seed of avocado, and then twist.

Scoop the flesh out using a metal spoon.

Put the milk powder (or milk), water, and brown sugar into the blender and blend it till smooth.

Ready to drink! This is another easy way besides using it to make salad and good for skin too.
