Measure the fabric length according to face size, the mask should cover the nose, mouth to the bottom of the chin

Spread the cloth, then add the coffee filter or tissue in it  

Fold into three folds, then insert a rubber or hair tie on both sides of the fabric

Fold the ends of the fabric exactly where the rubber is pinned, bringing one end together with the other so that the fabric is tight

Pull both the top and bottom ends of the mask

2. Sewing cloth mask

The following cloth mask is a mask with a rope model, suitable for women wearing hijab.

Material:

Needles and thread or sewing machine, if any  

Scissor  

Safety pins or clips to hold the fabric in place, if there is no paper clip can be replaced

Cotton fabrics, cotton shirts that are not used, or cotton pillowcases

Four strips of cotton fabric for binder with a length of about 45 cm, can be replaced with 4 laces of the same length

How to make:

Wash and dry the cloth first so that it is sterile when used

Measure the length of the fabric and cut into two rectangular sections, each measuring about 30x15 cm  

Differentiate the pattern of the front and back of the mask so they are not easily confused

Stack two rectangular fabrics, sew the back of the fabric facing forward, then sew each side of the layer until the fabric coalesces

Leave a little space at both ends of the fabric to slip the fabric strip or shoelaces

Slide the straps at each end of the fabric, holding them with pins or paper clips so the straps don't move

Sew all layers of fabric in a backstitch or repeat sewing so that the stitches are stronger in all parts  

Strap fabric mask is ready to use  

Make cloth masks effective this way

So far there has been no research that states cloth masks can hold the corona virus from spreading. However, as an emergency prevention effort, cloth masks must still be used with due regard to the following steps:

Use a mask by holding a strap or rubber, not the front of the fabric

Make sure your mouth, nose and chin are well covered

Cloth masks can only be worn for 4 hours, after that replace masks with clean cloth masks

Remove the mask by holding the rubber or string, discard the filter layer (tissue or coffee filter) if available, replace it with a new one each time you will use it again  

Cloth masks can be cleaned by washing with water and detergent in the washing machine or washing manually

After removing the cloth mask, do not directly hold the eyes, nose or mouth

Wash your hands with soap and running water, if not possible use a hand sanitizer right after removing the mask
