2. congratulating : Congratulation on your .....
    complimenting : What a/an [adjective] + [Noun] + [pronoun] + [tobe]
                                   example : What a beautiful girl she is!
                              How [adjective] + the + [Noun] + [tobe]

11. perbedaan antara complementing dan compleminting?​


Jawaban:

Sebagai kata kerja complement berarti “to complete” (melengkapi satu sama lain atau melengkapi sehingga menjadi sempurna, lebih baik, atau menarik), sementara compliment berarti “to praise (someone) or to say something nice about someone” (memuji atau mengatakan sesuatu yang baik tentang seseorang).


12. Definition congratulating and complementing


Jawaban:

Congratulating is a sentence that is used to congratulate someone because of their achievements

Complimenting is a sentence that is commonly used to compliment someone because of their looks, personality, voice,etc, that is in the positive way.
