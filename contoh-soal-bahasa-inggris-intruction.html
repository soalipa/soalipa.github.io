- Ardiansah Ibnu

- Raihan

Raihan: Hello There, What's Your Name?

(Halo Yang Disana, Siapa Nama Mu?)

Ardiansah Ibnu: Oh, My Name Is Ardiansah Ibnu. Just Call Me Ibnu.

(Oh, Nama Saya Ardiansah Ibnu. Panggil Saya Ibnu Saja)

Raihan: Hello, Ibnu! Where You From?

(Halo, Ibnu! Dimana Kamu Berasal?)

Ibnu: I'm From West Jawa, How About You?

(Aku Berasal Dari Jawa Barat. Bagaimana Dengan Mu?

Raihan: I'm From West Jawa too!. How old are you, Ibnu?

(Saya Berasal Dari Jawa Barat Juga! Berapa Umurmu Ibnu?)

Ibnu: I'm 12 year old (twelve year old)

(Saya Berumur 12 Tahun).

Raihan: Wow You Are Younger Than Me.

(Wow, Kamu Lebih Muda Dari Saya)

(1 detik kemudian)

Ibnu: Hey, the bell's ringing. See you later!

(Hey, Bel Sudah Berbunyi. sampai Jumpa Nanti!)

27. Karangan bahasa Inggris intruction to do something


Instruction to make an orange juice : 1. Prepare a mixer, an orange, sugar and ice cube. 2. Peel the orange and cut it into small pieces, then put it inside the mixer. 3. Pour 3 spoons of sugar and put all the ice cubes to make it fresh. 4. Close the mixer and turn on the mixer. Wait until the orange is blended well. 5. Open the mixer and pour the orange juice in a glass. Your orange juice is ready to serve !
