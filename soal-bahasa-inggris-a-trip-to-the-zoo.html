Jawaban = A. RECOUNT

Recount = Teks yang berkisah tentang pengalaman sesorang misal nya berlibur ke suatu tempat.

Trip to the zoo = Berwisata ke kebun binatang.

Berarti penulis menceritakan pengalaman dia berwisata ke kebun binatang.

39. 4. Ghifar wants to go the zoo.The correct negative sentence is ...A Don't Ghifar wants to go to the zoo.B. Doesn't Ghifar wants to go to the zoo.C. Ghifar don't want to go to the zoo.D. Ghifar doesn't want to go to thezoo​


Jawaban:

D. Ghifar doesn't want to go to the zoo

Jawaban:

D. Ghifar doesn't want to go to the zoo

Penjelasan:

Don't memiliki makna sama dengan do not, dan doesn't memiliki makna sama dengan does not.

Do dipakai untuk I, You, They, We.

Does dipakai untuk He, She, It, dan nama orang.
