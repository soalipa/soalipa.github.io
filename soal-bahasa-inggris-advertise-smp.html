32. 8. A: Do you think we should advertise it?B: Personally. I think that's a good idea.The underlined words express....A. ApologyAn opinionOffer helpAccept helpAgreement​


Expressing Opinions

JAWABAN:

Jawaban yang tepat untuk mengisi soal dibawah ini adalah option B. An opinion. Kalimat yang digaris bawahi merupakan uangkapan yang digunakan untuk menyatakan pendapat (opinion)

A: Do you think we should advertise it?

B: Personally I think that's a good idea.

The underlined words express....

=> B. An opinion

PEMBAHASAN:

An opinion is the way you feel or think about something. Our opinion about something or someone is based on our perspective. Whenever we give or express our opinion it is important to give reasoning or an example to support our opinion.

Asking Opinion

What do you think  …? Do you agree with me? Don’t you think(that) …? What  are you thought on that? Would you agree with me that …? Do you see what I’m getting at? Do you know /see what I mean? Etc..  

Giving Opinion

I think … To my mind … I (really) feel (that) … As for me, I reckon … I (honestly) think that … From my point of view  … In my opinion …/In my view … Personally speaking, I believe …
