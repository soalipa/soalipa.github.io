2. Cerita dal bahasa inggris yang terdapat preference


Jawaban:

Sophie has just been given some money from her mother. She decided to buy some snacks at the shop near the house. She took some time to find the best snack for her. She like eating chocolate better than eating cheese. So she took three bars of chocolate. On the way she went back to her house, she met a little kid sitting alone. She sat near him and asked why he sat alone. He said that he was so sad because he lost his money. He had plan to buy some chocolates for his mom as a birthday present. By hearing that story, Sophie decided something. She would prefer to give her chocolates to the kid rather than eat them all by herself.

Sophie baru saja diberikan sejumlah uang dari ibunya. Dia memutuskan untuk membeli beberapa makanan ringan di toko dekat rumah. Dia membutuhkan beberapa saat untuk menemukan makanan kecil yang terbaik untuknya. Dia suka makan coklat daripada makan keju. Jadi dia mengambil tiga batang coklat. Di dalam perjalanan kembali ke rumah, dia bertemu anak kecil duduk sendirian. Dia duduk dekatnya dan menanyakan mengapa dia duduk sendiri. Dia berkata kalau dia sangat sedih karena dia kehilangan uangnya. Dia punya rencana untuk membeli beberapa coklat untuk ibunya sebagai hadiah ulang tahun. Setelah mendengar cerita itu, Sophie memutuskan sesuatu. Dia akan memilih untuk memberikan coklat-coklatnya kepada anak itu daripada memakannya semua sendiri.

Penjelasan:

Preference adalah kalimat yang menyatakan memilih sesuatu dengan mengadakan perbandingan.

Formula:

Subyek + like + verb -ing + better than + verb -ing

Subyek + would prefer + to + verb 1 (base) + rather than + verb 1 (base)

Contoh:

I like going to the beach better than hiking on the mountain.

I would prefer to go to the beach rather than hike on the mountain.

3. merangkum materi preference ?​


RANGKUMAN MATERI PREFERENCE :

Preference artinya lebih suka. Jika kita harus memilih diantara dua hal yg keduanya sama - sama kita inginkan. Dalam konteks ketatabahasan (English Grammar). Preference harus menggunakan prinsip parallelism atau kesetaraan.

Cara membaca quick tips

Sesuaikan penggunaan item lebih suka dan daripada secara horizontal. Misalnya like dengan better tan atau prefer dengan to. Kalau kalian tertukar menggunakan Correlator. Misalkan prefer dengan than, atau like dengan to, maka bisa di pastikan salah !

Contoh :

» Most of teenagers in Bandung like' Black pink' better than 'Girls generation'.

» The staffs of this office like giving service to the costumer better than finishing administration.

» Australia tourists will prefer Kuta beachin Bali to Pattaya beach in Thailand.

10. apa fungsi dari preference?


Preferences (pilihan atau preferensi) bisa dipake ketika seseorang akan memberikan suatu saran, menawarkan sesuatu atau minta pendapat orang lain tentang apa yg seharusnya/lebih baik dilakukan.Preferences (pilihan/ preferensi) dapat digunakan ketika seseorang akan memberikan suatu saran, menawarkan sesuatu atau meminta pendapat orang lain tentang apa yang harus (lebih baik) dilakukan.
Kita sering menggunakan kata seperti: “prefer, would prefer, would rather” untuk menanyakan tentang pilihan (preferences).

18. berikan contoh soal preference dan jawabannya


Explanation:

Preference is a kind of expression which is used to show that someone wants something more than another thing or wants to do something more than another thing.

There are many kinds of words that you can use to express the preference:
- S + would rather + bare infinitive
Example: I would rather eat fried rice now.

- S + would rather + bare infinitive + noun + than + noun
Example: She would rather eat apples than oranges

- S + would rather + bare infinitive + than + bare infinitive
Example: I would rather stay at home than go out tonight

- S + would prefer to + bare infinitive + rather than + bare infinitive
Example: I would prefer to play rather than study

- S + would prefer to + bare infinitive + noun + rather than + bare infinitive + noun
Example: He would prefer to drink coffee rather than eat lunch

- S + had better + bare infinitive
Example: You had better go home

- S + had better + not + bare infinitive

Example: She had better not go to Japan


- S + prefer to + bare infinitive

Example: She prefers to go now


- S + prefer + nouns + to + nouns

Example: I prefer chocolate to vanilla


- S + prefer + gerund + to + gerund

Example: I prefer drinking tea to drinking coffee


- S + like + noun/gerund + better than + noun/gerund

Example: I like singing better than dancing


Answer:
