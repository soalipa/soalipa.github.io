Definisi Umum : “Danau”Menurut bahasa Indonesia adalah genangan air yang amat luas dan di kelilingi oleh daratan . Danau memiliki berbagai manfaat bagi kehidupan manusia , selain sebagai sumber air, danau juga dapat di fungsikan menjadi berbagai kegiatan ekonomi seperti irigasi,transportasi, perikanan, pembangkit listrik tenaga air (PLTA).

Deskripsi bagian : Danau toba adalah sebuah danau vulkanik dengan ukuran panjang 100 kilometer dan lebar 30 kilometer yang terletak di provinsi Sumatra Utara , Indonesia . Danau ini merupakan danau terbesar di Indonesia dan asia tenggara . di tengah danau ini terdapat sebuah pulau Vulkanik brnama Pulau Samosir.

Deskripsi Manfaat : danau Toba sejak lama menjadi tujuan wisata penting di Sumatra Utara selain Bukit Lawang , Beras tagi Dan Nias , Menarik wisatawan domestik maupun mancanegara








General Definition: "Lake" According to Indonesian language is a very large pool of water and surrounded by land. The lake has various benefits for human life, apart from being a water source, lakes can also be functioned into various economic activities such as irrigation, transportation, fisheries, hydropower plants.

Section description: Lake Toba is a volcanic lake with a length of 100 kilometers and a width of 30 kilometers located in the province of North Sumatra, Indonesia. This lake is the largest lake in Indonesia and Southeast Asia. in the middle of this lake there is a volcanic island named Samosir Island.

Description of Benefits: Lake Toba has long been an important tourist destination in North Sumatra in addition to Bukit Lawang, Rice Tagi and Nias, attracting domestic and foreign tourists

26. contoh soal tentang teks gambar atau caption dalam bahasa inggris beserta jawaban


Contoh soal teks gambar atau caption dalam bahasa inggris beserta jawaban adalah berikut ini. Tapi, sebelum menjawab pertanyaan mari pahami terlebih dahulu penjelasan dan pengertian dari caption. Caption sering kali kita dengar sebagai istilah populer dalam penggunaan sosial media. Definisi dari caption itu sendiri adalah sebagai serangkaian kata yang ditulis pada bagian bawah gambar, video, film, dll. Caption juga bisa diartikan sebagai potongan teks pendek yang terdapat dibawah gambar dalam sebuah buku, majalah, artikel yang menjelaskan tentang keadaan dan atau subjek pada gambar tersebut.


Setelah memahami pengertian caption, mari membuat pertanyaan dan jawaban mengenai caption.


(Gambar ada di bawah pembahasan)


Pembahasan

1. What is the purpose of the caption?

Answer: to give a description of what is going on in the picture


2. When was the picture taken?

Answer: it was taken on January 14 2016


3. Where was the location in the picture?

Answer: the location was in Thamrin, Jakarta


4. Who is the main subject in the picture that is described in the caption?

Answer: Indonesian President, Joko Widodo


5. What kind of caption is in the picture?

Answer: the kind of caption is cutline.

39. sangkuriang dalam bahasa inggris dan contoh soalnya


Once upon a time, a beautiful lady named Dayang Sumbi gave birth to a boy which she named Sangkuriang. When Sangkuriang was old enough to hunt, he took his mother’s dog Tumang (which according to this legend is an incarnation of a god and also Sangkuriang’s father) and ordered Tumang to chase after a boar. When Tumang did not follow Sangkuriang’s order, Sangkuriang became very angry and he killed Tumang. He carved out Tumang’s heart and brought it back to his mother.
Dayang Sumbi took the heart, cooked it and ate it. When she discovered that the heart belonged to Tumang, her husband, she was overcome with wrath. She sent her son away, but not before she hit him with a spoon and left a deep scar on his head.
Sangkuriang travelled around the world. After a long while, he arrived back in his village again without recognizing it. He saw a beautiful lady and fell in love with her. Little did he know that she was his own mother. He asked for her hand in marriage and she agreed.
Dayang Sumbi later realized that Sangkuriang was her son, as she recognized the scar that she had inflicted. She tried to tell him and break off the wedding, but he didn’t believe her and insisted to go on with the wedding. Dayang Sumbi then set an impossible condition that Sangkuriang has to fulfill in order to marry her: he should build her a big boat and a lake by damming Citarum river, all within one night, and it shall be finished by dawn. Sangkuriang agreed to the condition.
He built a boat from a large tree, and with the help of spirits, he dammed the Citarum river with landslides. The water eventually rose and filled the plain, turning it into a lake. When dawn was near, he was almost ready. Dayang Sumbi realized this, so she prayed for divine intervention. As an answer to her prayers, the eastern horizon lit up. Deceived by the lights, cocks crowed and farmers rose for the new day, thinking that dawn has broken.
Sangkuriang was also deceived. He thought his endeavor has failed. Angrily, he kicked the boat so that it toppled over. This boat became Tangkuban Parahu mountain (tangkuban means upside down, and parahu means boat). The pile of leftover woods became Mt. Burangrang, and the rest of the big tree became Mt. Bukit Tunggul. The lake became lake Bandung (which literally means ‘dam’).
