28. contoh main idea di akhir paragraf dalam bahasa inggris​


Penjelasan:

Berikut adalah beberapa contoh main idea atau gagasan utama yang bisa digunakan di akhir paragraf dalam bahasa Inggris:

1. In conclusion, the importance of education cannot be overstated as it provides individuals with the knowledge and skills necessary for personal growth and societal development.

2. In summary, the advances in technology have revolutionized various industries, leading to increased efficiency, convenience, and connectivity in our daily lives.

3. To sum up, the preservation of the environment is crucial for the well-being of future generations, and it is our collective responsibility to take immediate action towards sustainable practices.

4. All in all, effective communication plays a pivotal role in fostering strong relationships, resolving conflicts, and achieving success in both personal and professional spheres.

5. In a nutshell, a healthy lifestyle comprising balanced nutrition, regular exercise, and sufficient rest is essential for maintaining physical and mental well-being.

6. To conclude, the rise of social media has had a profound impact on society, altering the way we communicate, share information, and perceive ourselves and others.

7. In essence, cultural diversity enriches our society by fostering tolerance, understanding, and appreciation for different perspectives and traditions.

8. Ultimately, the pursuit of happiness lies in finding fulfillment and purpose in our lives, nurturing meaningful relationships, and embracing gratitude and self-acceptance.

Perlu diingat bahwa gagasan utama di akhir paragraf sebaiknya merangkum atau menekankan poin utama yang telah dibahas dalam paragraf tersebut.

29. kak akuminta tolong buatkan jawabannya tentang bahasa inggris. Tentang main idea dari keterang leeches!


Main idea biasanya berupa satu kalimat (atau lebih) yang mendasari sebuah paragraf. Main idea atau gagasan utama dapat ditemukan dari kalimat topik yang biasanya terletak di awal paragraf. Meskipun begitu, tak jarang kalimat topik (topic sentence) ini berada di tengah, akhir atau bahkan tidak secara jelas tertulis di dalam paragraf.

32. Soal tentang Hannah dn Alia dalam bahasa inggris


Jawaban:

How does Hannah contact Alia?

Hannah contacts Alia using *masukan bendanya*

ex: Hannah contacts Alia using her phone

atau bisa juga

Hannah contacts Alia by *masukan caranya*

ex: Hannah contacts Alia by calling her

Is anybody introducing Hannah to Alia?

No, nobody is introducing Hannah to Alia.

atau

Yes, *masukan siapa/masukan pronoun* is/are introducing Hannah to Alia.

ex:

Yes, Vina is introducing Hannah to Alia.

atau Yes, they are introducing Hannah to Alia

Penjelasan:

How does Hannah contact Alia = bagaimana Hannah mengontak Alia

Hannnah contacs Alia using/by ... = Hannah mengontak Alia dengan ...

note: pakai kata 'using' untuk menaruh kata benda dan 'by' untuk predikat

Is anybody introducing Hannah to Alia? = Apakah ada yang memperkenalkan Hannah ke Alia

No, nobody is introducing Hannah to Alia = Tidak ada yang memperkenalkan Hannah ke Alia

Yes, *masukan siapa/pronoun* is/are introducing Hannah to Alia = *masukan nama/pronoun* sedang memperkenalkan Hannah ke Alia

note: 'is' untuk subjek singular dan 'are' untuk subjek plural
