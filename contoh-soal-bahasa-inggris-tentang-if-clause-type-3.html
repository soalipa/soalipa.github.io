6. if clause type 1,2,3


If clause 1: If+S+Verb 1, S+will/can/may+ Verb 1
If clause 2:If+S+simple past, S+would/could/might+ bare infinitive
If clause 3:If+S+past perfect tense, S+would/should/could/might + have + past participle

Contoh Dialog Bahasa Inggris Type 2:

Person A: Hey, have you ever been to Bali?

Person A: Really? What did you do there?

Person B: Well, I visited some beautiful beaches like Kuta and Nusa Dua. I also explored the cultural sites like Tanah Lot Temple and Ubud Monkey Forest.

Person A: How long have you been playing?

Person B: I've been playing for about 10 years. I started when I was in high school and never stopped.

15. Contoh soal conditional clause dong yg type 1, 2 ,3 . 15 soal yaa sama jawabannya :D


Type 1
1) If Caroline and Sue ___(prepare) the salad, Phil ___(decorate) the house.
prepare/will decorate
2) If Sue ___(cut) the onions for the salad, Caroline ___(peel) the mushrooms.
cuts//will peel
3) Jane ___(hoover) the sitting room if Aaron and Tim ___(move) the furniture.
will hoover//move
4) If Bob ___(tidy up) the kitchen, Anita ___(clean) the toilet.
tidies//will clean
5) Elaine ___(buy) the drinks if somebody ___(help) her carry the bottles.
will buy//helps

Type 2
6) We ___(help) you if we ___(know) how.
would help//knew
7) My brother ___(buy) a sports car if he ___(have) the money.
would buy//had
8) If I ___(feel) better, I ___(go) to the cinema with you.
felt//would go
9) If you ___(go) by bike more often, you ___(be / not) be so flabby.
went//would not
10) She (not / talk) to you if she ___(be) mad at you.
would not//were

Type 3
11) If the goalie ___(jump) up, he ___(catch) the ball.
had jumped//would have caught
12) If the forwards ___(run) faster, they ___(score) more goals.
had run//would have scored
13) If it ___(have) a home game, our team ___(win) the match.
had been//would have won
14) If you ___(ask) me, I ___(help) you.
had asked//would have helped
15) If you ___(speak) English, she ___(understand).
had spoken//would have understood)

22. buatlah dialog tentang if clause type 0,1,2,dan 3​


Jawaban:

Contoh dialog conditional sentences type 0 (zero conditional):

Anne: What do you usually do on your holiday?

Rose: If I stay at home, I spend time with my sister.

Anne: You don’t go anywhere?

Rose: I go to the cinema if I my sister asks me.

Anne: What kind of movie you watch at cinema?

Rose: I always watch thriller whenever I go to cinema.

Anne: It sounds exciting.

Rose: It’s not exciting if my sister suddenly wants to watch another genre.

Contoh dialog conditional sentence type 1 (first conditional):

John: I can’t wait to see James at the final competition tonight.

Mark: Why?

John: If he wins, he will treat us next week.

Mark: Really?

John: Yes. Will you come tonight?

Mark: I will come if I get my father’s permission.

John: I will pick you up if you get your father’s permission, Mark.

Mark: Ok. Thanks, John.

Contoh dialog conditional sentence type 2 (second conditional):

Josh: My phone dropped when I got out of the car. I’m so relieved it’s not broken.

Ron: But the scratches are everywhere. If I were you, I would buy a new phone.

Josh: I would buy the new one if had more money.

Ron: What if you start saving?

Josh: I can’t since I just lost my job.

Ron: Don’t worry, Josh.

Josh: If I had a job, I would start saving.

Contoh dialog conditional sentence type 3 (third conditional):

Mary: Have you found your cat?

Kate: Yes, I have. I wouldn’t have found her if Shawn had not helped me.

Mary: What a relief! I’m sorry I couldn’t help you at that time.

Kate: It’s okay. By the way, how was holiday in France?

Mary: It was so exciting. If I my mother had not called me, I would have stayed longer.

Kate: Glad to hear that. I wish I had had money to go France with you.
