36. kalimat tunggal yg benar dalam bahasa Inggris "One year ago" apa "One years ago" ????


Jawaban:

The right one for saying the synonym of such adverb of time is 'one year ago', not 'one years ago'.

Pembahasan:

Adverb of time is a phrase to say certain time of an event uttered in a sentence in the English language. The phrase 'one year ago' is one example of adverb of time. It has the same meaning as 'last year'. In writing 'one year ago', you don't need to put 's' at the end of the word 'year' because the number before the word 'year' is 'one'. It means it is singular, not plural. The addition of 's' for a noun is only applicable for the plural one, not singular one. Here is the difference

'One month/ year/ week ago'   - singular

'Two months/ years/ weeks ago'   - plural

'Ten months/ years/ weeks ago'    - plural
