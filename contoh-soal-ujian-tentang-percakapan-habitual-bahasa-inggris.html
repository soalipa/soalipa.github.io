7. kak aku mau tanya ini. tolong bantuannya ya. ini tentang habitual action, command, or impossible condition​


1. He will get cold if the weather is bad (Conditional Type 1)

2.If it rained, I would take an umbrella. (Conditional Type 2)

3.John will help me if he can (Conditional Type 1)

4. If I were you, I should stay in a hotel (Conditional Type 2)

5. If he had known,he wouldn't have made a mistake (Conditional Type 3)

6.The teacher would have told you if you had asked him (Conditional Type 3)

7.I would buy that car if it were not expensive (Conditional Type 2)

8. We would have enjoyed the play if it hadn't been so long (Conditional Type 3)

9.If it is convenient, let's meet at 5 o'clock (Conditional Type 1)

10. If she were older, she would have more sense (Conditional Type 2)

Now study the fact below

1. Father were not at home yesteday, so I didn't get some money (Conditional Type 2)

2.I got a bad mark, so I didn't get gift from my parents(Conditional Type 2)

3.The class was dirty this morning,so we didn't use it for meeting.(Conditional Type 2)

4.I didn't get eat breakfast this morning, so I was very hungry (Conditional Type 2)

Conditional Type 1 biasanya:

If + Simple Present, Simple Future

Conditional Type 2 biasanya:

If + Simple Past Tense, Kalimat dengan menambahkan (Would) sesudah subjek

Conditional Type 3 biasanya selalu ditambahkan dengan had:

If + Past Perfect, Kalimat dengna menambahkan "Have + v3 atau Has + v3" sebagai klausa utamanya.

18. CONTOH HABITUAL ACTIONS ?


The present continuous tense is used to describe repeated, habitual actions that begin before a specific point of time (not necessarily the time of speaking) and finish after it. This point can be expressed with a time expression or a clause with a verb in the present simple tense:At 7.15, I'm having breakfast.
Whenever I get home, my dog is sleeping in the armchair.The present continuous is also used for repeated, habitual actions that continue for a period of time. This period of time can be expressed with a time expression or a clause with a verb in the present continuous tense:I'm having breakfast between 7 and 7.30.
While I'm walking the dog, my wife is making dinner.Daily activity~
I wake up at the morning, then take a bath. After that, i go to the kitchen to have breakfast. Finally i go to school ~~

35. Please make habitual sentences/simple present tense​


Jawaban:

Simple Present Tense

The simple present (juga biasanya disebut present simple atau present indefinite) adalah kata kerja yang digunakan untuk menunjukkan pengulangan, kebiasaan atau generalisasi (hal yang sudah umum terjadi). Simple present tense juga dapat digunakan untuk membicarakan tindakan terjadwal dalam waktu dekat dan dalam beberapa kasus yaitu keadaan yang terjadi sekarang.

Pembahasan

Buatlah 5 contoh kalimat dengan pola simple present tense

1.I study English two days a week

2.I do not go to school on Saturday

3.I play Badminton every Sunday

4.When does the she usually come home?

5.He never come back here since he was graduated from college

B. Fakta atau Generalisasi

1.We eat rice

2.Penguins do not fly

3.Cats hate dogs

4.Jakarta is in Indonesia

5.Tables are made of wood

C. Acara Terjadwal dalam Waktu Dekat

1.The bus leaves tonight at 6 PM.

2.The plane does not arrive at 11 AM, it arrives at 11 PM.

3.When do we board the plane?

4.The party starts at 8 o'clock

5.When does class begin tomorrow?

D. Sekarang (Kata Kerja Tidak Berlanjut)

1.He is not here now

2.She needs help right now

3.You have your passport in your hand

4.Do you have your passport with you?

5.Does she need help?
