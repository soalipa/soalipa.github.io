2. tips menjawab soal Listening Section bahasa inggris


memahami vocabularies nya1. Pahami bentuk-bentuk petunjuk (direction) pada masing-masing bagian (part) dengan baik sebelum hari-H ujian.
2. Bacalah pilihan pada masing-masing soal sebanyak mungkin ketika narator sedang membacakan perintah dan contoh soal (example).
3. Dengarkan dengan penuh konsentrasi dan fokuskan perhatian Anda pada percakapan yang sedang Anda dengarkan.
4. Maksimalkan kemampuan listening Anda pada soal-soal pertama pada masing-masing bagian.
5. Pelajari dan fahami bentuk dan jenis-jenis pertanyaan yang biasa muncul dalam tes

15. Buatlah story telling dengan tema story of sidoarjo dengan menggunakan bahasa Inggris​


Jawaban:

Once upon a time in the beautiful land of Indonesia, there was a small town called Sidoarjo. Nestled in the province of East Java, Sidoarjo was known for its rich history and vibrant culture.

Long ago, Sidoarjo was a quiet fishing village where the locals lived harmoniously with nature. The people relied on the bountiful sea for their livelihoods and shared a deep respect for the environment. However, destiny had something unexpected in store for this serene town.

In the year 2006, a sudden and catastrophic event occurred that would forever change the face of Sidoarjo. A massive mud volcano erupted in the area, spewing hot mud and gases into the air. The force of nature was unleashed, and a vast mudflow engulfed the surrounding villages and agricultural lands.

The eruption of the mud volcano, known as Lusi, brought devastation to the people of Sidoarjo. Homes, farms, and businesses were swallowed by the relentless mud, leaving thousands displaced and their lives shattered. It was a dark and challenging time for the community.

However, amidst the chaos and despair, the people of Sidoarjo displayed remarkable resilience and unity. They joined hands and worked tirelessly to rebuild their lives and their beloved town. Organizations, volunteers, and the government came together to provide support and aid to those affected by the disaster.

Over the years, Sidoarjo transformed itself from a town struck by tragedy into a symbol of hope and perseverance. The people embraced innovation and creativity to create new opportunities. They built new homes, revived their businesses, and developed alternative livelihoods.

Sidoarjo also became a center for art and culture, with its vibrant traditional dances, music, and festivals captivating visitors from near and far. The town's rich heritage and warm hospitality attracted tourists, who were inspired by the resilience and spirit of the people.

As the years passed, Sidoarjo continued to evolve and thrive. The mudflow became a part of its identity, reminding the people of their strength and the power of community. They established educational programs to raise awareness about environmental conservation and took measures to protect the remaining natural resources.

Today, Sidoarjo stands as a testament to the indomitable human spirit. It is a town that overcame adversity and emerged stronger than ever. The people of Sidoarjo are a living embodiment of resilience, reminding us that even in the face of the most challenging circumstances, hope can flourish and a brighter future can be built.

And so, the story of Sidoarjo teaches us that no matter how difficult the journey may be, the human spirit can triumph over adversity, and a community united can rebuild what was lost.

Once upon a time in the enchanting land of Indonesia, there existed a city called Kediri. Situated in the province of East Java, Kediri was a place rich in history, culture, and natural beauty.

Legend has it that Kediri was founded centuries ago by a wise and benevolent ruler named Sri Aji Jayabaya. Under his reign, Kediri flourished as a prosperous kingdom, attracting traders, scholars, and artists from far and wide.

The city was renowned for its magnificent architecture, adorned with intricately carved temples and palaces. The people of Kediri were known for their craftsmanship and skill in creating beautiful pottery, textiles, and traditional artwork.

However, like any kingdom, Kediri faced its share of challenges and struggles. Throughout its history, it witnessed the rise and fall of dynasties, the ebb and flow of power. But the spirit of Kediri endured.

One of the most significant events in Kediri's history was the arrival of the Majapahit Empire. Under the rule of King Hayam Wuruk, the Majapahit Kingdom expanded its influence, bringing prosperity and cultural exchange to Kediri. The city became a center of trade, attracting merchants from distant lands and fostering a diverse and cosmopolitan society.

As time went by, Kediri faced the test of resilience once again. It endured invasions and conflicts, but its people never lost hope. They stood united, protecting their beloved city and preserving their heritage.

In more recent times, Kediri transformed itself into a bustling modern city while still cherishing its rich cultural roots. The city embraced progress and development, with new industries and infrastructure shaping its landscape. Yet, amidst the urban hustle and bustle, the spirit of Kediri's past remained alive.

Today, Kediri stands as a testament to the harmonious blend of tradition and modernity. Its streets are adorned with colorful traditional markets, where locals and visitors alike can immerse themselves in the vibrant tapestry of Kediri's culture.

The people of Kediri continue to celebrate their heritage through annual festivals and events, showcasing their traditional dances, music, and culinary delights. The city has become a hub for artists, historians, and travelers seeking to explore its captivating past and discover the hidden gems within its walls.

As the sun sets over the majestic temples and lush landscapes of Kediri, one can't help but feel the enchantment and timeless beauty that permeates the air. The story of Kediri serves as a reminder that no matter the passage of time or the challenges faced, the spirit of a city and its people can endure, preserving the essence of its rich history and embracing the promise of a vibrant future.

And so, the story of Kediri teaches us that a city's true magic lies in the hearts of its people, who carry the legacy of their ancestors, breathe life into their traditions, and create a tapestry of stories that will be told for generations to come.

Over the centuries, Kediri has witnessed many changes and struggles, from the glory days of the Singhasari and Majapahit empires to the Dutch colonial era. However, the city has maintained its unique identity and charm. The city's strategic location, with access to major transportation routes, has made it an important trading center in East Java.

Despite facing various challenges, Kediri has remained resilient and determined to grow. It is a vibrant and dynamic city that offers something for everyone. Whether you want to explore its history and culture or experience its modern lifestyle, Kediri is an enchanting destination that will leave you with unforgettable memories.
