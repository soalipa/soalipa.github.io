32. jawab bahasa Inggris halaman 25 kurikulum 2018 kelas 8


Untuk menyelesaikan tugas ini, kamu harus mengerjakannya bersama dengan anggota kelompokmu. Kamu harus menanyakan anggota kelompokmu mengenai kemampuan apa yang mereka miliki dalam Bahasa Inggris, musik, olahraga, dan pekerjaan rumah. Setelah itu, kamu harus menuliskan data yang kamu peroleh setelah menanyakan anggota kelompokmu ke dalam tabel.

Berikut akan saya berikan contoh pertanyaan yang dapat kamu tanyakan ke teman sekelompokmu dan juga contoh kalimat yang dapat kamu tulis.

Questions

- What can you do in English, music, sports, housework?

- What can you not do in English, music sports, housework?

- Make sentences showing ability using can and can't.

Discussion

In order to ask someone's ability, we usually use the modal verb 'can'. There are two types of questions we can ask.

- Can you + verb + noun ?

For example:

A: Can you speak English well?

B: Yes, I can/No, I can't.

- What can you + verb + noun ?

A: What can you do in English?

B: I can read an English book well.

Answers

Let's take a look at these dialogues. I'll give one dialogue sample to fill in the table.

(2) This is a dialogue between me and my team member, Dina.

Me: Dina, can I ask you some questions about English?

Dina: Yes, sure.

Me: Can you read story in English?

Dina: Yes, I can.

Me: Cool. Next, can you answer questions in English?

Dina: Yes, I can. But I can answer only simple questions.

Me: Can you ask questions in English?

Dina: No, I can't.

Me: I have more questions. Can you play musical instrument?

Dina: Yes, I can.

Me: What can you play?

Dina: I can play piano and sing J-POP songs.

Me: Cool! What sports can you play?

Dina: I can play badminton.

Me: That's all? Can you do housework?

Dina: Yes, I can. I can bake a cake and cook rice.

Me: Last one, can you do other things?

Dina: Let me see. I can paint and write a story.

And then I will put the information I got from Dina to the table.

English:

- Play the piano

Others:

Conclusion

You can use the question pattern to ask your group mates about their ability to finish filling in the table. I hope the explanation is helpful for you. Good luck!
