18. Make dialog about holding and closing a phone conversation​


Person A: Hello?

Person B: Hey, it's me. I was just calling to see how you're doing.

Person A: Oh, hey there! I'm good, thanks for asking. What's up with you?

Person B: Not much, just wanted to catch up. Hey, can you hold on for a second? I've got another call coming in.

Person A: Yeah, no problem. I'll wait.

Person B: Thanks. (clicks over) Hello?

(Person A hears muffled conversation on the other end)

Person B: Hey, sorry about that. It was just a quick call I had to take care of. You still there?

Person A: Yeah, I'm here. No worries.

Person B: So, where were we?

Person A: I think you were just catching me up on what's been going on with you.

Person B: Right, right. So anyway, I was saying that I've been really busy at work lately...

Person A: (listening for a few minutes) Hey, I hate to cut you off, but I actually have to run. Can we catch up again soon?

Person B: Yeah, definitely. Let's talk again soon. Take care.

Person A: You too. Bye!

(Person B hangs up, and Person A presses the "end call" button on their phone)
