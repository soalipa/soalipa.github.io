19. Contoh kalimat dari kata preservation,unforgettable,destination,unlike. Masing masing 5 contoh


Answers

1. Preservation (noun): to keep something as it is or to keep something in a good condition.

Examples:

- We need a preservation area to protect some rare animals from extinction.

- This yogurt is very famous because the is kept in a special preservation box.

- Preservation of a national monument is very important.

- The new type of refrigerator has a good preservation technology. We can keep vegetables fresh for a week.

- I want to study about ancient preservation technique.

2. Unforgettable (adjective): something/someone that is difficult to forget. Creating a strong memory.

Examples:

- The view from the top of Mount Bromo is unforgettable.

- I want to hold an unforgettable birthday party.

- Her beautiful face is unforgettable.

- I'm sure that the meeting between Kim Jong Un and Trump will be unforgettable.

- Watching my favorite idol concert is an unforgettable experience.

3. Destination (noun): Goal, a place we want to go, end point.

Examples:

- It will take us 2 hours to reach our destination.

- My trip last week was very difficult but the view we saw at the destination was breath taking.

- We're going home together because we have the same destination.

- They've been so tired when they reached the destination.

- It's been two weeks since I sent the package but it hasn't arrived at the destination.

4. Unlike.

Unlike can serve as a preposition, an adjective, and a noun.

Unlike (preposition)

Example:

- She's very lazy, unlike her sister.

- He seems very angry these day, which is unlike him.

Unlike (adjective)

Examples:

- Boni and Doni are unlike, despite being twins.

- She is very unlike to other high school girls.

Unlike (verb)

Examples:

- I've unliked 30 of my friends' posts.

- Malfoy is unliked by many people because of his cold character.

30. Unforgettable experience​


Penjelasan:

Unforgettable Experience

One summer, my family and I decided to go on a road trip to explore a new destination. We packed our bags, filled the car with snacks, and set off on an adventure to a beautiful coastal town. Little did we know that this trip would become one of the most unforgettable experiences of our lives.

As we drove along the winding coastal roads, we were mesmerized by the breathtaking views of the ocean and the rugged cliffs. The salty breeze filled the air, and the sound of waves crashing against the shore created a soothing melody. It felt like we were in a paradise far away from the hustle and bustle of the city.

Upon arriving at our destination, we checked into a cozy beachside cottage. The place had a rustic charm with its wooden interiors and a stunning view of the sea from the balcony. We couldn't wait to start our exploration.

During the day, we spent our time on the sandy beaches, building sandcastles, and playing beach volleyball. The water was crystal clear and perfect for swimming and snorkeling. We were delighted to spot colorful fish and corals beneath the waves, making it a truly magical underwater world.

One evening, we decided to take a sunset boat tour. As the sun dipped below the horizon, the sky turned into a canvas of warm hues, creating a breathtaking panorama. Dolphins gracefully leaped alongside the boat, adding to the enchantment of the moment. It was a scene straight out of a dream.

As the night fell, we gathered around a bonfire on the beach, roasting marshmallows and sharing stories. The laughter and camaraderie created a sense of togetherness and happiness that was hard to put into words.

The next day, we embarked on a hike through a lush forest that led to a hidden waterfall. The sight of the cascading water amidst the verdant greenery was nothing short of awe-inspiring. We took a refreshing dip in the cool waters, feeling grateful for the beauty of nature that surrounded us.

As our trip came to an end, we were filled with a mix of sadness and gratitude. The memories we made together on this unforgettable journey would forever hold a special place in our hearts. It was a reminder of the beauty of life and the joy of experiencing new places and adventures with loved ones.

Even now, years later, we often reminisce about that summer road trip, and it never fails to bring a smile to our faces. It was more than just a vacation; it was a soul-nourishing experience that strengthened the bond between our family and left us with cherished memories to last a lifetime.
