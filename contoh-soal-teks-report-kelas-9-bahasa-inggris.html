13. Contoh teks descriptve, narative, recount dan report. dalam bahasa Inggris


Narrative Text

Generic structure:

1. Orientation

Yaitu berisi tentang pengenalan tokoh, waktu, serta tempat kejadiannya.

2. Complication

Yaitu gambaran masalah yang di alami oleh tokoh pada cerita yang harus dipecahkan.

3. Resolution

Yaitu bagaimana tokoh tsb menyelesaikan masalah.

Descriptive

Generic structure:

1. Identification : berisi tentang identifikasi hal / seorang yang akan dideskripsikan.

2. Description : berisi tentang penjelasan / penggambaran tentang hal / seseorang dengan menyebutkan beberapa sifatnya.

Recount Text

My Busy Day

Last Monday was a busy day for me. I spent my time to do a lot of activities from college to my home.

First, at the morning, I did my presentation’s assignment with my partner, she was Nurhidayah. It took 3 hours. And then we went to the campus for joining lecture. But, the lecturer said that our presentation would be started next week. It made us disappointed. The class was finished at 12.30.

After that, I had to go home because my grandmother was in a bad condition. She was hospitalized. So, it was a must for me to back home at that time. When I got there, there were so many members of my family. There were about 10 people. My aunt, my niece, my uncle and some of my cousins. We all hoped that our grandmother would get better soon.

Those activities made my day busy.

Generic structure:

1. Orientation: Menceritakan siapa saja yang terlibat dalam cerita, apa yang terjadi, dimana tempat peristiwa terjadi, dan kapan terjadi peristiwanya.

2. Events: Menceritakan apa yang terjadi (lagi) dan menceritakan urutan ceritanya.

3. Reorientation: Berisi penutup cerita/akhir cerita.

18. buatlah contoh teks report tentang teknologi beserta unsur kebahasaan dari teks tersebut dan beri contoh nya dari setiap unsur kebahasaan nya tersebut (dalam bahasa Inggris)​


Contoh teks report mengenai teknologi dalam bahasa Inggris adalah sebagai berikut.

Computers are a machine that handles information automatically. It can perform calculations and process data dan work with numbers to solve problems in a few seconds. It also can remember a great deal of information. So, it can be widely used in places, such as banks, offices, schools, courses, counters, or companies.

The computers work like calculators. Users have to command them to do this by pressing various buttons. To do the same work, users must give instructions to the computer. It calls a computer program. If we set up a program, the computer can provide a program and do all this work automatically without further help or instructions.

A computer also stores and handles numbers. The numbers in the computer may have some mathematical formulas or columns of figures. But, it may also have some codes that stand for letters of the alphabet, words, or any kind of instructions to the computer.

Pembahasan

Report text atau disebut juga dengan teks report merupakan sebuah laporan teks yang memberikan informasi mengenai suatu hal yang sebenarnya terjadi dari berbagai penelitian sistematis atau bersumber dari hasil analisis. Dalam ilmu bahasa Inggris, teks ini merupakan salah satu jenis yang dipelajari.

Dalam teks report di atas, unsur kebahasaannya adalah sebagai berikut.

Menggunakan Present Tense, contohnya dalam kalimat "computers are a machine that handles information automatically", "The computers work like calculators", dan "a computer also stores and handles numbers".Menggunakan General Noun, contohnya dalam kata computer, program, dan calculator.Menggunakan Action Verb, contohnya dalam kata perform, work, store, dan provide.Menggunakan Relating Verb, contohnya dalam kalimat "computers are a machine that handles information automatically" dan "So, it can be widely used in places, such as banks, offices, schools, courses, counters, or companies".Menggunakan Technical Language, contohnya dalam mathematical formulas, codes, alphabet, dan column of figures.

Covid-19

Covid-19 or Coronavirus Disease-2019 is an infectious disease caused by a new virus called SARS-Cov-2. This disease first identified in Wuhan, China, in the end of 2019. The virus widely spread over many countries and then in March 2020, WHO declared Covid-19 a pandemic.

This disease attacks respiratory system. Covid-19 also makes people who get infected experience several symptoms. The common symptoms are fever, dry cough, and tiredness. Some also experience aches, sore throat, diarrhea, conjunctivitis, headache, and loss of taste or smell. Meanwhile the serious symptoms are shortness of breath, chest pain, and loss of speech. However, there are many who do not experience symptoms.

It takes about 5-6 days to the symptoms to show. However, the incubation period takes 14 days. People who has mild to moderate illness usually can recover without hospitalization. Meanwhile those who experience serious symptoms have to seek medical attention.

The virus spreads through the droplets when infected people sneeze or cough. Therefore, WHO gives the way to prevent infection such as washing hands regularly with soap and water, avoiding touching face, covering mouth and nose while sneezing or coughing, staying at home, refraining from smoking, and practicing physical distancing.

Lunar Eclipse

The lunar eclipse is an event of the sun, the earth and the moon lie on one line so that the shadow of the earth covers a part or whole moon. The lunar eclipses that occur on this earth distinguished to several types, they are total lunar eclipse, partial lunar eclipse and lunar eclipse penumbra.

The process of the lunar eclipse begins when the earth is between the sun and the moon on the same line. The result is the sunlight can not reach the moon because it is blocked by the earth.

A total lunar eclipse is when the moon is right in the umbra (the darkest area). On a partial lunar eclipse, the earth is not entirely blocking the moon from the sun. While the other half of the surface of the moon is in the penumbra area. So there is still some sunlight that reaches the surface of the moon. On the lunar eclipse panumbra, all parts of the moon are in the penumbra. So the moon can still be seen with a gloomy color.

There is also a very rare lunar eclipse phenomenon called super total lunar eclipse red blood. This kind of lunar eclipse occurs due at the time of occurence of the total lunar eclipse. The moon position is in the position of the closest approach to the earth, thus making the moon seem larger and brighter than usual and at the same time only red color of sunlight penetrated to reflected by the earth’s atmosphere.

Jawaban:

Report text is a type of text that presents information from an observation, research, or study of objects, animals, people, or a place. The function or purpose of the report text is to describe the object as it is.

Penjelasan:

Report text adalah suatu jenis teks yang menghadirkan informasi dari suatu hasil pengamatan, penelitian, atau studi tentang benda, binatang, orang, atau suatu tempat. Fungsi atau tujuan dari report text adalah untuk menggambarkan objek apa adanya.

Generic structure

General Classification; Pernyataan umum yang menerangkan subjek laporan, keterangan, dan klasifikasinya.  Description: tells what the phenomenon under discussion ; in terms of parts, qualities, habits or behaviors; Pada bagian ini biasanya memberikan gambaran fenomena-fenomena yang terjadi; baik bagian-bagiannya, sifat-sifatnya, kebiasaannya, ataupun tingkah lakunya.
