2. tolong bantu saya cari contoh contoh kalimat pasten yang di ubah ke perfect dalam bahasa inggris


⇒Simple past tense diubah menjadi past perfect tense
contoh :
kalimat lansung : They said "we studied english last night"
K. tak langsung : They said that they had studied English the previous night

bonus :
⇒Present perfect tense diubah menjadi past perfect tense
contoh :
kalimat langsung : He had said "My brother has played football"
K. tak langsung : He said that his brother had played

my mother cooked my favourite foodRumus Past Tense.

Verbal

(+) S + V2 + O
(-) S + did + not + V1 + O
(?) did + S + O

Example : I studied math last night.

Nomina

(+) S + was / were + adj/adv
(-) S + was/were + not + adj/adv
(?) was/were + S + adj/adv

Example : She died yesterday.

24. apa rumus simpel pasten ? dan berikan contoh kalimat verbal dan nominal nya !


rumus simple past tense : Subject + Verb2 + object + etc... Ex : - i studied english last week - my father worked at seoul hospital last year.The Pattern of Simple Past Tense:

Subject + V2 + Object/ time signal ( for verbal )

Subject + to be (was/were) + object/ time signal ( for nominal )

Example:

Verbal: She sang
( "She" is the subject and "sang" is the v2 from sing )

Nominal: She was a teacher
( "She" is the subject, "was" is to be and "a teacher" is the object )

kurang lebih itu
simple past tense (verb)
(+) She took an examination last semester
(-) She didn't take an examination last semester
(?) Did she take an examination last semester?
simple past tense (noun verb)
(+) The manager was angry yesterday
(-) The manager was not angry yesterday
(?) Was the manager angry yesterday?

Past Continuous (Verb)
(+) He was studying when you came to his house last night
