An example of absolute measurement is the percentage of the amount of food consumed below an amount sufficient to sustain the human body's needs (approximately 2000-2500 calories per day for an adult male).
Relative poverty is a poverty that is not linked to the poverty line, this type of poverty comes from the perspective of each person, that is because the person feels poor because of comparing himself with the richer person.

18. Apa yang dimaksud dengan relative pronoun dalam bahasa Inggris


relative pronoun itu digunakan untuk menggabung 2 clause
digunakan juga untuk merujuk pada clause pertama
contohnya itu who, whom, which, whoever, whomever, whichever, atau that
contoh kalimatnya:
That guy who standing near the gate is my brother.
