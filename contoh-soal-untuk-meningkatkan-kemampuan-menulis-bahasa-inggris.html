6. membuat karangan bahasa inggris tentang cara meningkatkan kemampuan berbicara bahasa inggris


Now that English is a global language, it's very important for us to learn English as early as possible. Because the English language has different structures, forms and rules from the Indonesian language, it can be difficult at first to master this language. There are so many ways to improve your English, but in this writing, we will be focusing on the speaking skill first. Before we go into the steps on how to improve your English speaking skills, keep in mind that there are four stages of how people learn a language. First, they listen to the language, then they speak. After they're able to speak, they learn how to read. Finally, they learn how to write. It's important to remember these stages, because if you want to work on certain skill, you have to know which skill you need to master first.

Since we're talking about speaking skill, of course we need to master the listening skill first, according to the stages I have mentioned above. So, the first step to improve your English speaking skill is to listen a lot. By listening, you will learn pronunciation, tone of voice, intonation, syllable stress, and word stress, and all of those components are important in English language. Since you need to be able to speak with a native speaker, you also need to be able to understand what they say when they talk to you. You can listen people talk in English from podcasts, lectures, or even movies. You can also learn different accents, like American, British, or Australian.

Second, you need to practice. If you listen to a podcast or watch a movie at home, you can pause whenever you like it and repeat the words the speaker says. Try to imitate their way to pronounce the word, or even the accent as well! This is quite effective because your brain will record and memorize it. You can also repeat a whole sentence with different intonations and tones of voice to match with the context, for example when you're happy, confused or sad.

Third, talk to yourself in English. Yes, this may sound crazy and you may look crazy in front of your family or your friends. But it works! At first, it can be hard because you're not used to it. You will take time to translate everything you want to say from Indonesian to English. Remember, practice makes perfect. If you keep talking to yourself in English, your brain will get used to it and you don't have to feel like translating every words from Indonesian to English because you already think in English. You can find an interesting topic from the internet, and tell yourself what do you think of in English.

Finally, find a partner to talk to in English. You can ask your English to help you because they can correct your grammatical mistakes or pronunciation. You can also talk to your friend in English, so you and your friends learn English together. If there is an English club, that will even be better because you get to talk to some people in English. You can also talk to native speakers online, but be careful, find a trusted website when you do this because there are many evil people out there.

Speaking in English is not difficult when you know the right way to practice English. Of course, it takes time and a lot of practice, but you will thank yourself later. Don't be afraid of making mistakes. Remember, we are not native speakers and our first language isn't English, so it's okay to make mistakes. However, we should learn from them and avoid making the same mistakes twice.

Setelah pandai bahasa Inggris, dia menyisihkan waktunya untuk membantu adik kelasnya yang masih belum mampu dalam berbahasa Inggris. Pernyataan di atas merupakan wujud D. SYUKUR Fahrul atas kemampuan berbahasa Inggris yang dimilikinya berkat usah dan karunia Allah Swt. 

PEMBAHASAN

Syukur dalam Kamus Besar Bahasa Indonesia, diartikan sebagai rasa terima kasih kepada Allah swt, dan untunglah (meyatakan perasaan lega, senang dan sebagainya). Secara bahasa syukur adalah pujian kepada yang telah berbuat baik atas apa yang dilakukan kepadanya.

Dari maka itu Wujud Syukur Fahrul Atas Kemampuan Berbahasa Inggris yang dimilikinya Berkat Usaha Dan Karunia Allah Swt.
