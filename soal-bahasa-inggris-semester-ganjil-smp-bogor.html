10. bagaimana tulisan aksara sunda dari " SMP 4 Bogor"?


(Tulisan Aksara Sunda di bawah ini diketik dengan menggunakan unicode Aksara Sunda, jika smartphone atau perangkat anda belum support atau belum mendukung unicode aksara lokal, dapat dilihat pula dalam gambar yang terlampir di pembahasan ini)

ᮞ᮪ᮙ᮪ᮕ᮪ |᮴| ᮘᮧᮌᮧᮁ

SMP 4 Bogor

Pembahasan

Aksara Sunda Baku adalah bentuk modernisasi dari aksara sunda kuno, pada dahulu aksara sunda dituliskan dengan sistem abugida, yaitu sistem penulisan dari kiri ke kanan dan tanpa spasi, atau disebut juga dengan sistem scripto continua

Pada zaman sekarang, aksara sunda digunakan untuk menuliskan singkatan singkatan, seperti pada soal di atas, ada singkatan S + M + P dari kepanjangan sekolah menengah pertama. Pada aksara sunda kuno kita tidak dapat menulis singkatan singkatan seperti itu, namun pada aksara sunda baku ini, kita menerapkan penulisan singkatan yaitu dengan menulis aksara legena dengan ditambah rarangken pamaeh

Contoh: SMP = ᮞ᮪ᮙ᮪ᮕ᮪

Kemudian dalam penulisan angka, kita harus memberikan tanda pipa di kiri dan kanan, yang mengapit dari kedua sisi angka tersebut, hal ini untuk membedakan huruf biasa dengan angka

Contoh: 4 = |᮴|

Dan pada kata terakhir, untuk huruf mati berupa "r", kita tidak dapat menuliskannya hanya dengan aksara legena "r" yang diberi pamaeh, namun kita gunakan rarangken panglayar supaya dapat berbunyi "r"

Contoh: Bogor = ᮘᮧᮌᮧᮁ

Huruf r pada kata bogor tidak menggunakan huruf ra yang diberi pamaeh, melainkan kita menggunakan rarangken panglayar

30. deskripsi tentang kebun raya bogor dalam bahasa inggris 20 kalimat


a description of the botanical garden bogorBogor Botanical Gardens , tropical garden in Bogor, West Java,
Indonesia .

It is renowned for its research on regional flora.
The 215-acre (87-hectare) site was first used by the Dutch for introducing tropical plants from other parts of the world into the region. In 1817 it was converted into a botanical garden and served as a training ground for specialists in tropical botany. A large area of the original rainforest on which the garden was founded has been preserved in its natural state, providing excellent specimens for scientific study. The garden also has outstanding collections of palms, bamboos, cacti , orchids, and ornamental trees. It administers three other large gardens: the Cibodas Botanical Garden (founded 1862) in West Java, the Purwodadi Botanical Garden in East Java, and the Eka Karya Botanical Garden (1959) on the island of Bali.

The grounds of the Kebun Raya are
adjacent to a luxurious palace that is an occasional residence of the Indonesian president.

31. Contoh recount text bahasa inggris study tour ke junggleland bogor sentul beserta artinya


Subject : English
Category : Genre Based Writing - Recount Text
Level : SMP

Explanation :
Recount text is a text telling something that happen in the past. Constructing a recount text, you have to pay attention to the generic structure which is orientation (telling who, when and where), events and re-orientation (telling about the writer's comment of the story). And also in recount text we use simple past tense.

Answer:
This is the example of recount text "Study Tour to Jungle Land Bogor".
English version:

It took an hour from Jakarta. We arrived in Jungle Land at 10 a.m and we enjoyed playing some games offered by the Park.
         Firstly, We visited the Bird Park. There were many kind of birds in their own cage. Then, We went to the 4D Cinema to enjoy the feeling of watching movie using 4D glasses and the moving chair. After that, We went to the Snow World. It was a very tiring day but I was so happy. I hope someday, I could go there with my family and enjoyed some games that I had not tried yet.

37. descrption taman safari bogor dalam bahasa inggris​


Safari Park

Safari Park, which is largely known as “Taman Safari” by local people, is a fairly unique zoo. It’s about 90 km from the capital city of Indonesia, Jakarta and 2 km from Puncak. It is precisely located in Cisarua, Bogor, West Java.

This zoo is similar to a park which is located in Kenya, Africa. Even though it isn’t as large as Kenya Safari Park, just about 100 acres, but it still gives much enjoyment to the visitors. In most conventional zoos, the animals are put in cage, while Safari Park has the visitors in “cage”. They aren’t allowed to get off their vehicles because animals wander freely. Visitors who do not have car may make use of the exploring buses that has been provided by the park management.

The animals actually don’t wander freely to all over the zoo area, but they are just grouped into several different blocks. The first block is used for the wild carnivorous animals such as tigers and lions. The second block is used for big herbivorous animals such as elephants, rhinoceros, hippopotamuses, zebras, and giraffes. The third block is inhabited by primates like orang utans and gorillas. There are also many kinds of sheep and deer in it. There are bears, ostriches, and llamas in the last block. Each animal roams freely inside their own blocks.

The rest blocks are like in the conventional zoo that the animals are in cage, such as rare species of Indian white tigers, several kinds of monkeys, birds, and crocodiles. There are also some tame animals such as monkeys, bears, and baby tigers. The visitor can take pictures together with those animals.

This park also provides a playground. In this area, visitors are able to enjoy a circus show and buy some stuff from souvenir shop as memento. They are also able to enjoy wonderful small waterfall.
