8. Jelaskan dengan lengkap tentang Main Idea dan Supporting Details beserta contohnya.


Grasping the Main Idea:
A paragraph is a group of sentences related to a particular topic, or central theme. Every paragraph has a key concept or main idea. The main idea is the most important piece of information the author wants you to know about the concept of that paragraph.
When authors write they have an idea in mind that they are trying to get across. This is especially true as authors compose paragraphs. An author organizes each paragraph's main idea and supporting details in support of the topic or central theme, and each paragraph supports the paragraph preceding it.
A writer will state his/her main idea explicitly somewhere in the paragraph. That main idea may be stated at the beginning of the paragraph, in the middle, or at the end. The sentence in which the main idea is stated is the topic sentence of that paragraph.
The topic sentence announces the general theme ( or portion of the theme) to be dealt with in the paragraph. Although the topic sentence may appear anywhere in the paragraph, it is usually first - and for a very good reason. This sentence provides the focus for the writer while writing and for the reader while reading. When you find the topic sentence, be sure to underline it so that it will stand out not only now, but also later when you review.

22. Jawaban buku paket bahasa inggris kelas 9 kurtilas semester 2 hal 188Tentang 'NEIGHBOURHOOD' dicari main idea , detailed facts. butuh segera pls:)


Factual Reports

Factual report adalah teks yang bertujuan untuk memaparkan fakta umum tentang suatu obyek  secara obyektif. Dalam factual reports biasanya membahas tentang fakta dari benda, binatang, dan gejala alam dan sosial, yang diambil dan diadaptasi dari  sumber-sumber autentik.

Dalam factual report biasanya berisi tentang:

Untuk hewan maka terdapat:

1. Species: Berisi spesies hewan tersebut

2. Habitat: Berisi tempat tinggal/ hidup hewan tersebut

3. Habits: Berisi kebiasaan atau aktivitas hewan tersebut.

4. Physical Characteristic: Berisi tentang ciri fisik dari hewan tersebut.

5. Emotional Characteristic: Berisi tentang ciri sifat/ emosi hewan.

6. Function: Berisi fungsi hewan tersebut.

Untuk Benda maka terdapat:

1. Class/ Definition: Berisi tentang Kelas/ definisi dari hal tersebut.

2. Materials/ Elements: Berisi tentang benda tersebut terbuat dari apa/ elemen dari hal tersebut.

3. Characteristic: Berisi ciri-ciri benda tersebut

4. Function: Berisi fungsi benda tersebut.

Pembahasan

Factual Report dari Neighborhood

MAIN IDEA  

Definition  

1) A family is a group of people who live together  in the same house.  

2)  Neighborhood is a group of families who live together in a specific area.

DETAILED FACTS  

Elements

1) A Neighborhood consists of a group of families who live in same area.  

2)  They live in same area and help each other. They are usually called Rukun Tetangga  (RT) in Indonesia.

Characteristics  

Neighborhood help and share for each other.

Functions  

1) With the neighborhood we feel safe and happy.  

2) Having neighborhood means our family doesn't live alone.

3) Each member of neighborhood will help and share for each.

4) They usually make an organization called Rukun Tetangga for formal purpose.
