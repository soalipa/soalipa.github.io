1. Specify the set of completion of: |5x - 6| - 4 = 10Soalnya pake B inggris :/


|5x - 6| - 4 = 10

|5x - 6| = 10 + 4

|5x - 6| = 14

5x - 6 = 14

5x = 14 + 6

x = 20/5

x = 4


5x - 6 = -14

5x = -14 + 6

x = -8/5


Maka, HP = { -8/5, 4 }
