40. I will put my idol posters..... the wall.... my bedroom. The suitable preposition to complete the sentence is ..A. on - aboveB. in - onC. above - inD. on - in​


(D) On - in

PEMBAHASANPreposition

Yaitu kata yang menunjukkan letak suatu benda.

Above = Di atas

Below = Di bawah

On = (tepat) di atas

In = Di dalam
