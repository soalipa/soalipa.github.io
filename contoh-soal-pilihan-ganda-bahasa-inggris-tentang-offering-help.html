2. contoh 10 soal pilihan ganda tentang offering help beserta jawabannya


Offering Something (Menawarkan Sesuatu)

Ini adalah ungkapan yang disampaikan untuk menawarkan suatu barang, bantuan, atau pertolongan kepada orang lain. Dalam bahasa Inggris, contoh ekspresi yang sopan untuk menawarkan sesuatu kepada orang lain adalah:

* Your bag looks heavy. May I help you carrying it? (Tas Anda kelihatannya berat. Bisakah saya bantu membawanya?)

* Would you like a cup of hot tea? (Anda mau secangkir teh panas?)

* I have just bought a good book, would you like to read it too? (Aku baru saja beli buku yang bagus, kamu ingin membacanya juga?)

Sedangkan Offering Help adalah menawarkan yang secara spesifik tentang bantuan yang bisa diberikan pada orang lain. Misalnya:

* Is the English homework difficult? Let me help you doing it. (Apakah PR Bahasa Inggrisnya sulit? Akan kubantu mengerjakan)

4. buatlah dialog bahasa Inggris tentang offering help yang menggunakan bahasa informal​


Jawaban:

Here's a dialog in informal English about offering help:

A: Hey, I noticed you're carrying a lot of books. Need some help?

B: Oh, that's so kind of you! Yeah, these books are pretty heavy. Thanks for offering!

A: No problem at all! Let me take some of them for you. Where are you headed?

B: I'm heading to the library, it's just around the corner.

A: Great! I can carry a few books for you. By the way, do you need help with anything else?

B: Actually, I'm trying to find a café nearby. Do you know any good ones?

A: Absolutely! There's a nice café just down the street. I can walk you there if you'd like.

B: That would be awesome! Thanks for being so helpful.

A: You're welcome! I'm always happy to lend a hand. Let's go grab some coffee and have a chat along the way.

Note: In informal conversations, people tend to use contractions and more casual language. The dialog above reflects this casual tone and the friendly nature of offering and accepting help.
