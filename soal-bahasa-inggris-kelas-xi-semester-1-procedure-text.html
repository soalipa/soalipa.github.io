How to Make a Fried Rice

Ingredients

3 cups cooked white rice2 spoons of cooking oil1 cup uncooked carrots, shredded 1/2 cups frozen green peas, thawed2 large eggs, lightly beaten1 cups uncooked scallions, sliced, divided 1/4 cups low sodium soy sauce, or to taste

Steps

1. Coat a large nonstick skillet with cooking oil; warm pan over medium-high heat. Add eggs; tilt pan so that eggs cover bottom. 2. When eggs start to set, break them up into pieces with a heat-proof spatula. Cook until eggs are cooked through, about one minute more; remove eggs from skillet and set aside.

3. Off heat, recoat same skillet with cooking spray; set over medium-high heat. Add carrots and all but 2 tablespoons scallions; sauté until carrots are crisp-tender, about 3 or 4 minutes. 4. Stir in cooked rice, peas and soy sauce, cook until heated through, stirring once or twice, about 1 minute. Gently stir in cooked egg and remaining scallions; heat through. Yields about 3/4 cup per serving

22. persamaan dan perbedaan procedure text dan manual procedure (text dalam bahasa Inggris) ​


Jawaban:

PERSAMAAN (equality/sameness)

1. General Purpose (tujuan umum): Both procedure text and manual procedure have the general purpose of providing instructions or guidance on how to perform a specific action or process.

2. Similar Structure (struktur serupa): They both have a similar structure, consisting of steps or a sequence of actions that must be followed by the reader or user.

DIFFERENCE (perbedaan)

1. Content and Expertise (konten dan keahlian): Procedure Text is for everyday tasks like cooking or personal care and is easy to understand. Manual Procedure is for technical or professional tasks, often requiring specialized knowledge.

2. Context (konteks): Procedure Text is used in everyday situations, while Manual Procedure is used in technical or industrial settings where precision and safety are critical.

Kalimat ke-4

Conditional type 2: I would be smart if you taught meIn fact: I will not be smart because you don't teach me

Kalimat ke-5

Conditional type 2: I would teach you how to learn English if my mother allowed meIn fact: I will not teach you how to learn English because my mother doesn't allow me

Penjelasan:

Kalimat conditional berupa kalimat yang mencirikan sesuatu yang tidak berdasarkan fakta, melainkan fakta berbeda dengan kondisi yang ada.

Penggunaan:

S + would + V1 + ROS + if + O + V2 + ROSIn fact + S + will + not + V1 + ROS + because + O + don't/doesn't + V1 + ROS

ROS = rest of sentence (O + C)

Contohnya:

I would go if you accompanied me, in fact I will not go because you don't accompany me

I would go hanya suatu kata yang diharapkan agar semoga obyek dapat menemani subyek pergi tapi pada kenyataan subyek tidak akan pergi karena obyek tidak menemaninya

Kesimpulan:

Conditional sentence merupakan kalimat yang mencirikan seseorang terhadap sesuatu yang bukan berdasarkan fakta. Sesuatu yang diambakan harap dapat terjadi pada kenyataan. Tapi, itu semua berdasarkan kondisi/keadaan yang ada dimana subyek belum tentu bisa mendapatkan/melakukan apa yang ada di pikirannya

27. cari tentang procedure text lengkap dalam bahasa inggris


The purpose of a procedure is to tell the reader how to do or

make something. The information is presented in a logical

sequence of events, which is broken up into small sequenced

steps. The most common example of a procedural text is a

recipe.

Types of Procedural Texts

Texts that instruct how to do a particular activity ; recipes, rules

for games, science experiments, road safety rules, how to do it

manuals. Texts that instruct how to operate things how to operate

an appliance, a machine, the photocopier, the computer

Features A procedure usually has four components

Goal or Aim -states what is to be done

Materials, Ingredients or Equipment- listed in order of use, includes items needed

to complete task

Steps—a series of steps

Evaluation or Conclusion- how the success of the procedure can be tested

5.How to Make Ice Cream

Materials:

2 cups milk or cream

2 tablespoons sugar

1 teaspoon Vanilla extract

Several cups of Ice

1 cup salt

You need these supplies to make ice cream

Tub or large coffee can to hold salt and ice

Glass bowl or small coffee can

Electric mixer or whisk

Ice cream scoop

Storage container for freezer

Steps:

First, setting up the cooling apparatus. Stir ice and salt in the larger container.

Then, combine the ingredients. Mix together the milk, sugar, and vanilla in the glass bowl. Then, set this bowl in the tub filled with ice and rock salt, making sure that the salt water does not spill into the bowl.

Next, mix the ice cream. Mix the ingredients in the small container vigorously. The salty ice mixture will cool the mixture down until it turns into ice cream. It should take about 10 to 15 minutes to stir the ice cream using either method. Stir until it is free from ice crystals and is the right consistency.

Last, storage and serving. ice cream is ready.

How to Make a Fried Rice

Ingredients

3 cups cooked white rice2 spoons of cooking oil1 cup uncooked carrots, shredded 1/2 cups frozen green peas, thawed2 large eggs, lightly beaten1 cups uncooked scallions, sliced, divided 1/4 cups low sodium soy sauce, or to taste

Steps

1. Coat a large nonstick skillet with cooking oil; warm pan over medium-high heat. Add eggs; tilt pan so that eggs cover bottom. 2. When eggs start to set, break them up into pieces with a heat-proof spatula. Cook until eggs are cooked through, about one minute more; remove eggs from skillet and set aside.

3. Off heat, recoat same skillet with cooking spray; set over medium-high heat. Add carrots and all but 2 tablespoons scallions; sauté until carrots are crisp-tender, about 3 or 4 minutes. 4. Stir in cooked rice, peas and soy sauce, cook until heated through, stirring once or twice, about 1 minute. Gently stir in cooked egg and remaining scallions; heat through. Yields about 3/4 cup per serving
