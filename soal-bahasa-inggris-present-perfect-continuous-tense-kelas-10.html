9. kalimat present tense past tense present continuous past continuous present perfect past perfect future tense be going to,modal 1,modal2 active dan passive menggunakan kata invite dalam bahasa Inggris ​


Jawaban:

Berikut ini adalah contoh kalimat-kalimat yang menggunakan kata "invite" dalam berbagai bentuk tense dan voice dalam bahasa Inggris:

Present Tense (Simple Present):

- Active: She invites her friends to the party.

- Passive: The guests are invited to the party.

Past Tense (Simple Past):

- Active: He invited me to his wedding last year.

- Passive: I was invited to his wedding last year.

Present Continuous:

- Active: They are inviting guests for the charity event.

- Passive: The guests are being invited for the charity event.

Past Continuous:

- Active: We were inviting people to join our club when it started raining.

- Passive: People were being invited to join our club when it started raining.

Present Perfect:

- Active: The company has invited a renowned speaker for the conference.

- Passive: A renowned speaker has been invited for the conference.

Past Perfect:

- Active: She had invited all her relatives to the family reunion before she left.

- Passive: All her relatives had been invited to the family reunion before she left.

Future Tense (be going to):

- Active: We are going to invite our neighbors to the barbecue party next weekend.

- Passive: Our neighbors are going to be invited to the barbecue party next weekend.

Modal Verb 1 (can):

- Active: Can you invite Sarah to the meeting tomorrow?

- Passive: Can Sarah be invited to the meeting tomorrow?

Modal Verb 2 (should):

- Active: You should invite your parents to the graduation ceremony.

- Passive: Your parents should be invited to the graduation ceremony.

14. kak tolong kasih tau contoh kalimat positif negatif dan introgatifnya passive voice dari Present continuous tense, Present perfect tense, Present perfect continuous tense, Simple past tense, Past continuous tense, Past perfect tense, Past perfect continuous, dunggg


Jawaban:

Berikut adalah contoh kalimat positif, negatif, dan interrogatif dalam bentuk passive voice dari beberapa tense yang Anda sebutkan:

1. Present Continuous Tense (sekarang sedang terjadi):

- Positif: The cake is being baked by my sister. (Adik perempuan saya sedang memanggang kue.)

- Negatif: The book is not being read by the students. (Buku tidak sedang dibaca oleh para siswa.)

- Interrogatif: Is the car being washed by the mechanic? (Apakah mobil sedang dicuci oleh montir?)

2. Present Perfect Tense (terjadi di masa lampau namun memiliki dampak di masa sekarang):

- Positif: The house has been renovated by the new owners. (Rumah telah direnovasi oleh pemilik baru.)

- Negatif: The report hasn't been submitted by the team yet. (Laporan belum diserahkan oleh tim.)

- Interrogatif: Have the invitations been sent by the event organizer? (Apakah undangan telah dikirim oleh penyelenggara acara?)

3. Present Perfect Continuous Tense (terjadi di masa lalu dan masih berlanjut hingga sekarang):

- Positif: The project has been being worked on by the team for several months. (Proyek telah dikerjakan oleh tim selama beberapa bulan.)

- Negatif: The garden hasn't been being watered regularly. (Taman tidak selalu disiram secara teratur.)

- Interrogatif: How long has the patient been being treated by the doctor? (Berapa lama pasien telah dirawat oleh dokter?)

4. Simple Past Tense (terjadi di masa lalu):

- Positif: The movie was watched by millions of people. (Film tersebut ditonton oleh jutaan orang.)

- Negatif: The letter wasn't sent by John. (Surat itu tidak dikirim oleh John.)

- Interrogatif: Was the game played by the children yesterday? (Apakah permainan itu dimainkan oleh anak-anak kemarin?)

5. Past Continuous Tense (sedang terjadi di masa lampau):

- Positif: The car was being repaired by the mechanic when I arrived. (Mobil sedang diperbaiki oleh montir saat saya tiba.)

- Negatif: The phone wasn't being used by anyone when it rang. (Telepon tidak sedang digunakan oleh siapapun saat berdering.)

- Interrogatif: What were you doing when the accident happened? (Apa yang sedang kamu lakukan ketika kecelakaan terjadi?)

6. Past Perfect Tense (terjadi sebelum suatu waktu di masa lampau):

- Positif: The package had been delivered by the courier before I got home. (Paket telah dikirim oleh kurir sebelum saya pulang.)

- Negatif: The task hadn't been completed by the deadline. (Tugas belum selesai sebelum batas waktu.)

- Interrogatif: Had the tickets been booked by the travel agent when you called? (Apakah tiket sudah dipesan oleh agen perjalanan ketika kamu menelepon?)

7. Past Perfect Continuous Tense (terjadi sebelum suatu waktu di masa lampau dan masih berlanjut):

- Positif: The team had been working on the project for weeks before they finally completed it. (Tim telah bekerja pada proyek tersebut selama berbulan-bulan sebelum akhirnya menyelesaikannya.)

- Negatif: The plants hadn't been being watered regularly before they withered. (Tanaman tidak selalu disiram secara teratur sebelum layu.)

- Interrogatif: How long had they been waiting for the bus before it arrived? (Berapa lama mereka telah menunggu bus sebelum bus itu tiba?)

Semoga contoh-contoh di atas dapat membantu Anda memahami penggunaan passive voice dalam berbagai tense yang disebutkan.

Jawaban:

Tenses

1. Simple Present Tense

Verb :

(+) S + V1 (s/es)

(-) S + Do not/ Does not + V1

(?) Do/Does + S + V1 + ?

Non Verb :

(+) S + Am/Is/Are + Adjective/Noun

(-) S + Am not/Is not/Are not + Adjective/Noun

(?) Am/Is/Are + S + Adjective/Noun + ?

2. Present Continuous Tense

Verb :

(+) S + Am/Is/Are + Ving

(-) S + Am not/Is not/Are not + Ving

(?) Am/Is/Are + S + Ving + ?

We can’t use to be in continuous tenses

3. Present Perfect Tense

34. jelaskan tentang present perfect tense dan present continuous tense​


Jawaban:

Present Perfect Tense :

— Kejadian lampau

— Kejadiannya sudah lama terjadi atau ada hubungannya dengan kejadian sekarang.

S + have/has + past participle

Contoh : She has seen the movie before

Present Continuous Tense :

— Kejadiannya sedang terjadi sekarang atau sedang berlangsung

S + am/is/are + V ing

Contoh : He is reading a book

Jawaban:

Present Perfect Tense

Tense ini digunakan untuk menunjukan aktivitas yang sudah berlangsung di masa lampau tapi masih berlanjut hingga masa kini atau sudah berhenti pada titik tertentu di masa lampau, namun efeknya masih terasa. Tense ini punya ciri-ciri yaitu menggunakan have/has + verb 3 (past participle).

Rumus kalimat:

(+) Subjek + have/has + verb 3(-) Subjek + have/has + not + verb 3(?) Have/has + subjek + verb 3

Contoh kalimat

(+) I have eaten a banana(-) I have not eaten a banana(?) Have I eaten a banana?

Present Continuous Tense

Tense digunakan untuk menunjukan aktivitas yang sedang berlangsung pada masa sekarang. Tense ini punya ciri-ciri yaitu menggunakan verb berakhiran -ing.

Rumus kalimat:

(+) Subjek + to be + verb-ing (-) Subjek + to be + not + verb-ing(?) To be + subjek + verb-ing

Contoh kalimat:

(+) I am playing a video game(-) I am not playing a video game(?) Am I playing a video game?
