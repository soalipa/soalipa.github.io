2. menceritakan my pet it is kitten dalam bahasa inggris beserta artinya


As far back as I can remember there have always been cats in my life. When I was growing up there were always a cat around the house and sometimes kittens too. My brother and I used to love playing with the kittens, they were so cute and cuddly. We often had dogs too, but that's another story. Later on when I got married and we bought our first house, the first thing we did was to go out and buy a kitten. Her name was Kinky!


Kinky was a tortoise shell female cat and she was so lively and funny. She used to dash round the house and do cartwheels and her tail would go all kinky (hence her name). My parents lived a 10 minute walk away and when we visited them we would always take Kinky with us. My husband just used to carry her in his arms and she seemed to love it. My mum was cool with us bringing the kitten round but my dad wasn't too keen but he was ok about it in the end.

When she got bigger we had to stop taking her. Some time later we decided to get another cat to keep Kinky company so we bought a male cat, he was white with black patches and a bit grumpy so we called him "Boss". He wasn't as lively as Kinky and seemed quite lazy by comparison.

B : hi A ! i am fine, thanks
A : did you finished your art homework ?
B : not yet, i don't know what must i draw
A : me too, but i think we could draw an animal because the teacher gave us "Favorite Animal" theme for our homework
B : so, have you think about what will you draw ?
A : i think i will draw my pet, Tito
B : Tito ?
A : he is a Persian cat that have been joined my family since i was ten, and now i am twelve
B : so he is two years old ?
A : yeah
B : what made you like Tito ?
A : you know, Tito is different from other cat. He tend to understand the situation, when i was sad he will come to me and being around me as he want to entertain me and that's worked, i was not sad anymore
B : magic
A : yeah, i like him because he has a very soft fur as soft as a doll, he has a very big stomach too as big as a pregnant cat, even if he won't pregnant
B : draw a cat is difficult, isn't ?
A : i said that Tito is different, he also have a different in his physical appearance, for example, his face is round as a ball, his eye is round too, and he has a round stomach too because he loves eating and became fat from time to time, that's why i will not face any difficult when i want to draw him
B : you're very lucky to have a magic pet like Tito
A : you're right, i love him so much
