4. apa rumus dari simple future dan future continous. beserta contoh!


simple future tense :
. Verbal Sentences :
 (+) S+Will+V1+o (-) S+Will+not+V1+o (?) Will+S+V1+o Example : (+)  She will meet me tomorrow.
 (-)  She will’ not meet me tomorrow
(?)  Will she meet me tomorrow ? 
future continus tense :
Formula’s : (+) S + will + be + v-ing+ O
(-) S + will not +be + V- ing + O
(?) Will + S+ be + V-ing + O Example : (+)  He will be teaching in my house tomorrow. (-)  He will not be teaching in my house tomorrow (?)  Will he be teaching in my house tomorrow ?
. 




Rumus simple future : sebject+will/shall+verb1+object
contoh : we shall go to jakarta tomorrow.
Rumus future continous : subject+be+verb-ing+object
contoh : she will be cleaning the floor

5. Buat fungsi simple past tense dan future tense, rumusnya ,contoh kalimatnya (+, _, ?) Dan contoh soal pilihan ganda 4 (2 past tense, 2 future tense)​


Jawaban:

Simple past tense untuk membicarakan aksi yang terjadi pada satu waktu tertentu di masa lampau

future tense untuk membicarakan aksi yang terjadi pada satu waktu tertentu di masa yang akan datang.

rumus:

simple past

+ ) s + v2+ O.

-) s + did + v1 + O

?) did + s +v1 + O ?.

simple future

+ ) s + will/shall +v1 + O

- ) s + will/shall+not+v1+O

? ) will/shall+s+v1+O ?.

contoh :

simple past tense

+ ) i ate rice , yesterday( saya makan nasi kemarin)

-) i did eat rice, yesterday(saya tidak makan nasi kemarin)

? ) did i eat rice,yesterday ?.(apakah saya makan nasi kemarin?)

simple future

+ ) i will eat rice tomorrow ( aku akan makan nasi besok)

- ) i wil not eat rice tomorrow(aku tidak akan makan nasi besok)

? ) will i eat rice tomorrow ?.(akankan aku makan nasi besok?)

note: untuk penggunaan "will" ketika subjek kalimat berbentuk (i,you,we,they,she,he,it,dan nama orang lebih dari satu nama ex: doni and riza)

untuk penggunaan shall (I,she,he,it,dan nama orang tunggal ex : doni )

8. contoh kalimat future of obligation beserta penjelasan ny


future obligation adalah tata bahasa yg digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu kegiatan yang sudah diatur untuk dilakukan di waktu tertentu dimasa depan. Artinya sama dengan "must" tapi future obligation digunakan bila kegiatan yg dimaksud sudah direncanakan sebelumnya.

rumusnya

be + to infinitive

be: am, is, are, was, were, been
infinitive: kata kerja bentuk dasar

contoh: I am to travel by plane to Barcelona

artinya: saya harus pergi ke barcelona dengan pesawat.

kepergiannya dengan pesawat sudah direncanakan.

berbeda dengan "I must go to barcelona". Dalam kalimat ini, kepergiannya ke barcelona dilakukan secara spontan.

18. contoh dialog dua orang menggunakan future tense/ future perfect beserta permasalahannya


SIMPLE FUTURE/FUTURE PERFECT TENSE

Jawaban

Pendahuluan  

Sebelum kita membuat contoh dialog 2 orang menggunakan simple future/future perfect tense, perlu kita pelajari lebih dahulu fungsi serta rumus simple future/future perfect tense sehingga kita bisa menyusun dialog dengan benar.

Simple Future Tense berfungsi untuk menceritakan peristiwa yang akan terjadi, menyatakan akibat perbuatan tertentu dan memohon/meminta seseorang melakukan sesuatu.  

Rumus yang digunakan adalah :  

Subject + will/shall + verb 1 (kalimat positif)  

contoh: (+) Ramona will move to Bali tomorrow morning.  

Subject + will/shall + not + verb 1 (kalimat negatif)  

contoh: (-) She will not move to Bali next week.  

Will/shall + Subject + verb 1 (kalimat tanya)  

contoh: (?) Will she move to Bali tomorrow morning?  


Future Perfect Tense berfungsi untuk menyatakan peristiwa atau perbuatan yang akan telah terjadi atau akan telah selesai dilakukan di waktu tertentu di masa datang/depan.

Rumus yang digunakan adalah :

Subject + will + have + verb 3 (kalimat positif)  

Subject + will + not + have + verb 3 (kalimat negatif)  

Will + Subject + have + verb 3 (kalimat tanya)  


Pembahasan  

Contoh dialog 2 orang menggunakan simple future/future perfect tense

Pedro, Martha and Lisa are classmates. Lisa will hold a birthday party this evening. Both Pedro and Martha are invited to the party. They are having a conversation about coming to Lisa's party together.

Pedro : Hi Mart, what’s up? Will you come with me to Lisa's birthday party this evening?

Martha : Hi P. Sure, I will come with you after the volley ball practice. When will the party begin?


Pedro : Great! It will begin around 7 p.m. When will your practice have finished? I will pick you up.


Martha : My practice will have finished at 6 p.m.


Pedro : I will pick you up at 6 p.m. then.

Martha : I will not make it. I will need a couple minutes to get ready. Can you pick me up around 6.30 p.m.?


Pedro : Of course, I will be there at 6.30.

Martha : Cool! See you later, P. Thanks a lot.


Pedro : See you, Mart! You are welcome.

Catatan:

Kalimat yang digarisbawahi pada dialog dalah contoh kalimat menggunakan simple future tense

Kalimat yang dicetak tebal pada dialog adalah contoh kalimat menggunakan future perfect tense

Rumus Simple Future Tense
1. Positif
Subject + will/shall + verb I + object
Subject + am/is/are + going to + verb I + object
Contoh :
We will go to school soon
The students are going to have a test next month
2. Negatif
Subject + will/shall + verb I + object
Subject + am/is/are + not + going to + verb I + object
Contoh :
We will not go to school soon
The students are not going to have a test next
3. Pertanyaan
Will/shall + subject + verb I + object
Am/is/are + subject + going to + verb I + object

Will we go to school soon ?

37. rumus present tense dan future tense beserta contohnya


Simple present tense
Rumus :
(+) Subject + Verb 1 + Object
Example : We go to school by bus
(-) Subject + Do/Does + Not + Verb 1 + Object
Example : We do not go to school by bus
(?) Do/Does + Subject + Verb 1 + Object
Do you go to school by bus?

Simple Future
A. Simple Future Verbal
(+) Subject + will/shall +Verb1 +object + keterangan
B. Simple Future Nominal
(+) Subject + will/shall + be + nominal (adjective, adverb of place, job)

Example
(+) Nadia will be a Junior High School student next year
(-) Nadia will not be a Junior High School student next year
(?) Will Nadia be a Junior High School next year?


Semoga membantu, jangan lupa follow ya.. Present Tense
1). Simple present
(S + (am/is/are) + O) atau (S + V1 + (e/es) + O)
We eat rice everyday

2). Continous
(S + (is/am/are) + V1 + ing + O)
She is cleaning the room

3). Perfect
(S + have/has + been + O) atau (S + have/has + V3 + O)
He has done since morning

4). Perfect Continous
(S + have/has + been + V1 + ing + O)
I have been working in Jakarta since 2004

Future Tense
1). Simple future
(S + shall/will + V1/to be + O)
I shall go to visit my friend tomorrow

2). Continous
(S + shall/will + to be + V1 + ing + O)
My father be going to garden tomorrow morning

3). Perfect
(S + shall/will + have/has + been + O)
They will have worked as salesman next month

4). Perfect Continous
(S + shall/will + have/has + to be + V1 (ing) + O)
Gito will have been studying Math for a week by next month
