6. MAPEL : BAHASA INGGRISMATERI : APPLICATION LETTER KELAS : XII SMA____________________TOLONG DIBANTUKETERANGAN : • PERTANYAAN ADA DILAMPIRAN ATAS !• KALAU MENJAWAB HARUS DISERTAKAN DENGAN PENJELASAN ! [ TERIMA KASIH ]​


APPLICATION LETTER

1) The applicant's address

2) Date

3) The recipient's contact information

4) Salutation

5) Introduction

6) Main body and relevant work experience

7) Conclusion

8) The complimentary close (sign off) and signature.

EXPLANATION

A job application letter is one of business letters, contains correspondence between an applicant and organization or companies that publish a job vacancy.

Generic structure

1) The applicant's address

It contains the applicant's contact information. It is placed on the left hand-side of the paper, towards the top.

2) Date

It contains the date when the applicant writes a letter.

3) The recipient's contact information

It contains the recipient's information (name of the responsible officer, title of the responsible officer, name and address of the organization).

4) Salutation

It contains regards (words of greeting) to the recipient. The salutation in a business letter (a job application lettee) is always formal.

For example:

Dear Mr. Paterson (if the contact is a man's name)

Dear Mrs. Dew (if the contact is a woman's name)

5) Introduction

It contains statement of the post or a job are the applicant is interested in, the reason for making personal-referral, and how the vacancy is known.

6) Main body and relevant work experience

It contains a summary of the applicant's background and critical skills that make him/her qualified for the job position.

7) Conclusion

It contains the applicant's thanking to the person who considerates the application letter, the applicant's expectation, the applicant's availability for the job, and where can be contacted.

8) The complimentary close (sign off) and signature.

It contains the warm regards, courteous words to end the letter and the apllicant's signature.

Dear, Mr. and Mrs. Firman

Your friend, Mr. Herlambang tells us that you are looking for the food catering. You are confused in serving the healthy foods for your children at home during your business trip across country, Malaysia.

The Catering Enterprise is the correct choice to solve your problem. Do you want us to take care of your children ? We have many variety of delicious foods. We pay attention for the food hygiene and our food is cooked with natural preservatives. We attached the food and beverage catalogue for the daily menu. Would you like to see it in the enclosed file?

In order to keep body health, we have the professional exercise trainer. He can train the children in the morning. Do you want us to train your children? It is good for your children health. If you are interested, we can easily do it for you.

Well, Mr. and Mrs. Firman to promote our business, we offer the affordable price for both services, the food catering and the exercise trainer. So, you don't have to worry about the finance ptoblem. Besides, we always serve our customers as well as we could. Should you need any further information about The Catering Enterprise, please feel free to contact us.

Yours faithfully,

Caesalpiniadew

EXPLANATION

The letter above is one of personal business letters, because the letter is sent for the customer, business matters.

The social Function of the letter is exchanging the information to the recipient

Generic Structures of a letter above are

a. The heading. It contains the date and the writer’s email address at the beginning of letter.

b. Salutation. It contains regards to the recipient, such as : Dear ....

c. The introduction, It contains the writer’s background of writing the letter.

d. The body of letter/content of the letter. It conveys the writer’s detailed information.

e. The closing/closure. It contains the farewell expression used to end the conversation through the letter include complimentary phrases, such as sincerely yours, best regard, with love and etc

f. Signature, it contains the writer’s name and the writer’s autograph.

Language featuress of a letter are

a. It uses the simple present tense  

Simple present tense is used to talk about the reality/daily activity/personality/quality.

b. It uses the indirect speech, the repetitive conversation from the first speaker.  

c. It uses the simple past tense, it tells about the past event/experience.

d. It uses the passive voice to reveal the important information

15. contoh 10 soal pilihan ganda tentang offering help beserta jawabannya


Offering Something (Menawarkan Sesuatu)

Ini adalah ungkapan yang disampaikan untuk menawarkan suatu barang, bantuan, atau pertolongan kepada orang lain. Dalam bahasa Inggris, contoh ekspresi yang sopan untuk menawarkan sesuatu kepada orang lain adalah:

* Your bag looks heavy. May I help you carrying it? (Tas Anda kelihatannya berat. Bisakah saya bantu membawanya?)

* Would you like a cup of hot tea? (Anda mau secangkir teh panas?)

* I have just bought a good book, would you like to read it too? (Aku baru saja beli buku yang bagus, kamu ingin membacanya juga?)

Sedangkan Offering Help adalah menawarkan yang secara spesifik tentang bantuan yang bisa diberikan pada orang lain. Misalnya:

* Is the English homework difficult? Let me help you doing it. (Apakah PR Bahasa Inggrisnya sulit? Akan kubantu mengerjakan)

EXPLANATION

Conditional sentence adalah kalimat pengandaian/bersyarat, di mana suatu keinginan dapat terwujud apabila persyaratannya terpenuhi. Conditional sentence terdiri dari 2 klausa/kalimat, satu kalimat sebagai If clause dan kalimat lainnya sebagai main clause/result clause. If clause menggambarkan kondisinya, dan main clause menggambarkan konsekuensinya.

Conditional tipe 1 [First conditional] menjabarkan keinginan yang kemungkinan terjadi saat ini ataupun nanti-nanti (real possibility) berdasarkan prediksi. [True in the present/future]

Rumus

If+S+verb1(s/es)..., S+will+verb1......

Example

If I have some money, I will buy a new book

Conditional tipe 2 [Second conditional] menjabarkan keinginan yang kemungkinan kecil terwujud atau diragukan terjadi saat ini atau mendatang. [Untrue in the present/future]

Nominal sentence pada conditional tipe 2 menggunakan tobe "were" untuk subyek tunggal dan jamak.

Rumus :

If+S+verb2 ......, S+would+verb1......

Example

If I were you, I would tell the truth

Conditional tipe 3 [Third conditional] menjabarkan keinginan yang tidak terwujud di waktu lampau. Pengulasannya saat ini hanyalah sebagai dampak dari kejadian di waktu lampau. [Untrue in the past]

Rumus :

If+S+had+verb3... S+would+have+verb3....

Example

If I had come to your house, your father would have been happy.
