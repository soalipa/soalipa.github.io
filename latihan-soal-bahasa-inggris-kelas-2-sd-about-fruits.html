10. write a brief analytical exposition about the importance of eating vegetables and fruits regularly​


The important of eating vegetables and fruits regularly

I personally think that eating vegetables and fruits regularly is important in our daily life. Why do I say so?

Fruit and vegetables are a good source of vitamins and minerals, including folate, vitamin C and potassium. They're an excellent source of dietary fibre, which can help to maintain a healthy gut and prevent constipation and other digestion problems. A diet high in fibre can also reduce your risk of bowel cancer.

Fruits are an excellent source of essential vitamins and minerals, and they are high in fiber. Fruits also provide a wide range of health-boosting antioxidants, including flavonoids. Eating a diet high in fruits and vegetables can reduce a person's risk of developing heart disease, cancer, inflammation, and diabetes

Eating vegetables every day is important for health. They provide essential vitamins, minerals, and other nutrients, such as antioxidants and fiber. Research consistently shows that people who eat at least 5 servings of vegetables a day have the lowest risk of many diseases, including cancer and heart disease.

Based on the facts above, it is obvious that everyone needs to eating vegetable every day.  In other words, we can say eating vegetable and fruit is truly important in our life.

Penjelasan:

Analytical exposition ialah salah satu jenis teks yang mempunyai fungsi untuk menginformasikan kepada pembaca tentang bahasan atau masalah yang ada tanpa menggiring atau mempengaruhi pembaca untuk setuju.

Tujuan teks analytical exposition adalah untuk meyakinkan pembaca bahwa masalah yang dibahas memang penting untuk di diskusikan bersama. Maka dari itu penulis sering menambahkan pertanyaan di akhir teks, seperti "Do you agree with this topic?"

Generic structure dari teks analytical exposition adalah

1. Thesis

Thesis adalah topik yang akan di diskusikan. Dalam thesis bisa ditambahkan alasan agar pembaca bisa memprediksi mengapa kita mengangkat topik tersebut.

2. Arguments

Di bagian argument, penulis dapat menjelaskan lebih rinci lagi tentang topik yang dibahas.

3. Reiteration/Conclusion

Bagian ini terletak dibagian terakhir atau yang biasa disebut dengan kesimpulan. Dalam kesimpulan, penulis memberikan sebuah penegasan kembali mengenai topik yang diangkat dalam tulisan kita.
