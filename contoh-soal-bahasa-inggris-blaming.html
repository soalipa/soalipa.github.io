30. tolong buatkan dialog bahasa inggris 3 orang dengan menggunakan expressions admitting, promising, blaming, accusing


Explanation:

Expression of admitting is a kind of expression that is used when someone admits that he/she is the one who makes a mistake.
Here are some expression of admitting that you can use:
- Yes, I did
- I'm sorry, it is my mistake
- Yes, I admit what I have done is wrong

Expression of promising is a kind of expression that is used when we want to make a promise to someone.
Here are some expression of promising that you can use:
- I swear that...
- I promise you that ...
- I can assure you that ...

Expression of blaming is a kind of expression that is used when someone blames another person for a mistake happened.
Here are some expressions of blaming that you can use:
- It's your fault
- I think you are the one who could have done it
- You are the one to blame

Expression of accusing is a kind of expression that is used when someone said that another person is the one who makes a mistake although he/she doesn't know whether it's right or wrong
Here are some expressions of accusing that you can use:
- It's ... fault
- It must have been ... fault
- ... must be the doer
