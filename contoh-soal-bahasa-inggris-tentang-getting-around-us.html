On the corner, there is a fan. There is a clock at the the wall. There is not a cupboard in my bedroom, but there is a bookshelf near the fan. I love my bedroom very much. B: “Yes, I will. What is it about?”

A: “It is about our friend, Dina. Listen to me!”

B: “Okay.”

A: “Dina asked me to join her trip next week.”

B: “And then?”

A: “I am confused, because next week I will have my first text in my course.”

B: “I see. Then?”

A: “How to refuse Dina’s offer?”

B: “Just say the truth.”

A: “Okay I will try.”

B: “Anyway, look what I’ve got here!”

A: “Why?”

B: “What do you think about my new motorcycle?”

A: “Wow! That is a cool motorcycle! Next time I will borrow it from you.”

B: “Sure you can.”

Things around us:

Badu: May I borrow your bicycle, Budi?

Budi: Yes, but what happened with your bike?

Badu: My bike is broken, the chain is broken off, it has got flat tires.

Budi: I am sorry to hear that, Badu.

Badu: That is okay, my uncle is fixing it now. By the way, may I also borrow your helmet, socks, shoes and ray-ban glasses?

Budi: Sure, here they are.

Badu: Thank you, you're my lifesaver, Budi!

Budi: Don't mention it.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban merupakan materi pembelajaran Things Around Us (benda-benda di sekitar kita) yang juga merupakan bagian dari Vocabulary (kosa kata / perbendaharaan kata) dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Things Around Us dalam percakapan tersebut:

Bicycle / bike = sepeda

Tires / Tyres = ban

Chain = rantai (sepeda)

Helmet = helm (pelindung kepala)

Socks = kaos kaki

Shoes = sepatu

Ray-ban glasses = kacamata riben (pelindung mata)

Benda-benda di sekitar kita:

Badu: Bolehkah aku meminjam sepedamu, Budi?

Budi: Ya, tapi apa yang terjadi dengan sepedamu?

Badu: Sepedaku rusak, rantainya putus, bannya kempes.

Budi: Aku turut prihatin mendengarnya, Badu.

Badu: Tidak apa-apa, paman saya sedang memperbaikinya sekarang. Ngomong-ngomong, bolehkah aku juga meminjam helm, kaus kaki, sepatu, dan kacamata ray-banmu?

Budi: Tentu, ini dia.

Badu: Terima kasih, kau penyelamatku, Budi!

Budi: Jangan sungkan ya.
