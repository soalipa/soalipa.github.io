19. Contoh dialog bahasa inggris expression of suggestion and advice 4 personal ? yang panjang ? adakah ?


Contoh dialog bahasa inggris menggunakan expresion of suggestion dan advic untuk 4 orang :

Henry: Hi Tina, why are you so sad?
Tina: I broke my mom's phone last night. I haven't told her about it yet. What should I do?
Henry: You should try to fix the phone first. Do you know Jeremy? He is good at fixing things. Why don't you ask for his help?
Tina: That sounds good!. Thanks for your suggestion Henry!
Tina: Hi Jeremy! I heard from Henry that you are good at fixing things. Do you mind fixing this phone for me?
Jeremy: I don't mind at all. Let me see the phone.
Tina: here you go (gives the phone to Jeremy)
Jeremy: (checking the phone).. Ok...I know what's wrong with this phone. The motherboard is broken and you need to get it replaced.
Tina: Is it easy to find a replacement?
Jeremy: Yes, but it will be expensive. It will cost you 2000000 rupiah.
Tina: oh no that's expensive! I can't afford that! what should I do?
Jeremy: I would suggest you to buy a new and cheaper phone instead. If you don't have enough money, you can also try to find a part time job.
Tina: but I need to replace the phone now. Otherwise my mom will kill me.
Jeremy: In that case, why don't you confess now to your mom. You can promise her that you will replace the phone eventually.
Tina: I guess so, I have no other options, Haven't I?

Tina goes home.....

Mom: Welcome home Tina. How was school?
Tina: Hi mom, school was fine.
Mom: What's wrong? why are you frowning?
Tina: Mom.. I have something to tell you...
Mom: what is it Tina?
Tina: I broke your phone. I'm so sorry.. I promise that I will get you a new phone.
Mom: It's okay Tina, but how are you going to replace the phone?
Tina: I will find a part time job and use the money to buy you a new phone.
Mom: Awww. what a sweet girl. I think my little girl has grown up. I really appreciate that Tina. I'm so proud of you for being responsible and able to admit your mistake. 
Tina: Thanks mom. I promise I will be more careful next time. 
Mom: Ok, in the meantime, why don't we go and eat lunch in the dining room?
Tina: it sounds good mom

An example of identify is a woman watching a movie and feeling the pain felt by a woman character in the movie. Identify means to determine who or what someone or something is. An example of identify is finding out what species a flower belongs to.

Daisy

To state your identity in English, basically you only need to say your personal information like name, address, age, nationality and interest. As for the next ones, which is talking about favourite day and memories, you pick one of your favourite, if you have more than one. Here's the example:

40. jelaskan apa itu personal participant dalam bahasa Inggris, terimakasih ​


Jawaban:

"Personal participant" refers to an individual who is personally involved or participating in an activity or event, such as a conference or seminar, without representing a specific organization or company. The term is often used to distinguish individual participants from those who are representing an organization.

Penjelasan dalam Bahasa Indonesia:

"Personal participant" dalam bahasa Inggris mengacu pada seseorang yang terlibat atau berpartisipasi secara pribadi dalam suatu aktivitas atau acara, seperti konferensi atau seminar, tanpa mewakili organisasi atau perusahaan tertentu. Istilah ini sering digunakan untuk membedakan partisipan pribadi dari partisipan yang mewakili sebuah organisasi.
