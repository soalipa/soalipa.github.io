33. Tolong di bantu tugas bahasa inggris Sudah saya kasih rumusnya tapi saya tidak mengerti Soal pada gambar ke 4 exercise


Conditional Sentence Exercises (Gambar ke 4)


1. I (would have gone) if I had known. (CS type 3)

2. If my car had not broken, I should have caught the train. (CS type 3)

3. If she were older, she (would have) more sense. (CS type 2)

4. If you (read [v2]) that book carefully, you would understand it. (CS type 2)

5. If the children (are) good, they can stay up late. (CS type 1)

6. I (would buy) that hat if it were not so clear. (CS type 2)

7. You (will kill) yourself if you always work as hard as that. (CS type 1)

8. If they had waited, they (would have found) me. (CS type 3)

9. We (would have enjoyed) the play better if it had not been so long. (CS type 3)

10. I should have come yesterday if I (had had) nothing to do. (CS type 3)


Catatan:

(1) Pada kalimat #10, terdapat dua "had" ("...if I had had nothing to do."). "had" yang pertama merupakan "had" yang ditambahkan dalam pengandaian (CS type 3), sedangkan "had" yang kedua merupakan kata kerja/verb yang sebenarnya. (2) Kalimat #2 kurang masuk akal apabila dikatakan dalam dunia nyata.

Penjelasan:

Subjective pronoun

Pronoun ini digunakan untuk subyek yang diterangkan pada kalimat demi menghindari pengulangan kata. Kata ganti subyektif ini memfokuskan pada subyeknya

Kata ganti subyektif:

Contohnya:

I talk with you

I berperan sebagai subyeknya, maka kata gantinya bersifat subyektif

you know herthey think I'm smartwe owe youhe likes cupcakeshe is funnyThe cat is cute. It makes me want to hug itLove your book. It is cool

Objective pronoun

Pronoun ini digunakan untuk memfokuskan obyek yang sedang dibicarakan atau berada pada subyeknya. Dimana kata ganti obyektif ini bukan berupa subyek lagi tapi obyek

Kata ganti obyektif:

meyouthemushimherit

Contohnya:

You talk to me

Me berperan sebagai obyeknya, maka kata gantinya bersifat obyektif dimana you sebagai subyeknya

she knows youdon't let them run awayjust hand this on usI think she likes himAlbert is the one for herThe cat is cute. It makes me want to hug it

Possessive pronoun

Pronoun ini digunakan untuk menerangkan obyek yang dimiliki oleh siapa. Dimana kata ganti kepemilikan ini menunjukkan pada pemilik dari obyek pada kalimat

Kata ganti kepemilikan:

myyourtheirourhisherits

Contohnya:

You have borrowed my book

**Possessive Adjective: Their**

**Demonstrative Adjective: That**

**Quantifier: Several**

Once upon a time, in a quiet neighborhood, there was a lovely house. This house belonged to the Johnson family. It was their dream home, and they had spent several years turning it into a warm and inviting place.

The Johnsons loved their house because of its charming features. It had a white picket fence, that made it stand out in the neighborhood. That was not the only thing that caught the attention of the passerby. The beautiful garden in front of their house was their pride and joy. Inside, the house had several spacious rooms. The living room was their favorite, where they spent many cozy evenings together as a family. The big windows in that room allowed plenty of natural light to enter.

The Johnsons had several memories in their lovely home. They celebrated birthdays, anniversaries, and many holidays in their charming house. It was a place filled with laughter, love, and warmth. The house was not just a building; it was their sanctuary, their refuge, and the place where their hearts truly belonged.
