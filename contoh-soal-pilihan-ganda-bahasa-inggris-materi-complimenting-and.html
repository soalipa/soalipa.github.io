Jawaban:

Sure, here's an example conversation involving compliments, thanking, showing care, and congratulations among four people:

Person A: Hey, I just wanted to say that your presentation today was really impressive, Person B. You explained everything so clearly.

Person B: Thank you so much, Person A! I really appreciate your kind words. I was a bit nervous, but I'm glad it came across well.

Person C: By the way, Person D, I heard you've been feeling under the weather. How are you feeling now?

Person D: Thanks for asking, Person C. I'm still recovering, but I appreciate your concern. It means a lot to me.

Person A: Also, I wanted to congratulate you, Person C, on your recent promotion. That's a huge achievement!

Person C: Thank you, Person A! I'm really excited about it and grateful for the opportunity.

Person B: And speaking of achievements, Person D, I heard you won the photography contest! That's fantastic!

Person D: Oh, thank you, Person B! I'm really thrilled about it. Your support means a lot to me.

Person C: Well, it's great to have such supportive friends like all of you. Let's continue to lift each other up.

Person A: Absolutely! We're here for each other through thick and thin.

Person B: Agreed. Let's keep the positive energy flowing!

Person D: Thanks, everyone. Your words and friendship mean the world to me.
