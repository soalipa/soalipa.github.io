11. contoh teks mendeskrisipkan angin dalam bahasa inggris


Foehn wind or local wind or waterfall wind is the wind that occurs when there is movement of air masses climb a mountain with a height of over 200 meters. Air masses reach the top of the mountain will undergo condensation and eventually arise rain on one side of the slope. As for the other slope does not occur rain hindered the high mountains. Areas that do not experience the so-called rain rain shadow area.

In the rain shadow area of the wind over the mountains will move down the mountain slope at high speed. It led to rising temperatures, because every down 100 meters of air rises 1 ° C. Thus wind down is hot and dry. The wind that is called Foehn wind.

Foehn wind that occurs in Indonesia are as follows:

    1 Wind Bahorok (Deli, North Sumatra)
    2 Wind Beetles (Cirebon, West Java)
    3 Wind Gending (Pasuruan, East Java)
    4 Wind Brubu (Makassar, South Sulawesi)
    5 Wind Wambraw (Biak, Irian Jaya)
