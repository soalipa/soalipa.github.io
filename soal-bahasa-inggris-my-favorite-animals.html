Jawaban:

My Favorite Teacher

My favorite teacher is my English teacher. His name is Felix. He is such an inspiring teacher as he always teaches us patiently and attractively. He is one of the best teachers I have ever met.

He really understands his students’ need. Mr. Felix often teaches us in some funny ways. He is so creative in preparing teaching learning activities in the class. His writing is so good that we always admire it. Every time he notices that his students have been so tired to study, he loves to tell his inspiring experiences in his life to encourage us.

Guru Favorit Saya

Guru favorit saya adalah guru bahasa Inggris saya. Namanya Felix. Dia adalah guru yang menginspirasi karena dia selalu mengajar kita dengan sabar dan menarik. Dia adalah salah satu guru terbaik yang pernah saya temui.

Dia benar-benar memahami kebutuhan murid-muridnya. Pak Felix sering mengajari kami dengan beberapa cara yang lucu. Dia sangat kreatif dalam mempersiapkan kegiatan belajar mengajar di kelas. Tulisannya sangat bagus sehingga kami selalu mengaguminya. Setiap kali dia memperhatikan bahwa murid-muridnya begitu lelah untuk belajar, dia suka menceritakan pengalaman inspirasinya dalam kehidupannya untuk mendorong kita.

Answer: 

To state your identity in English, basically you only need to say your personal information like name, address, age, nationality and interest. As for the next ones, which is talking about favourite day and memories, you pick one of your favourite, if you have more than one. Here's the example:

I'm Tugay Karan. I'm an librarian, 28 years old and from Turkey. I live in Ankara. I like football and reading. My favourite day in a week is Monday, which is my day off from work and I can go to any other libraries in the city because it's the working day. It's a day off in my workplace, The Irish Library and Culture Centre, because our busiest days are on weekends, while Monday is our most quiet day of the week. As for my favourite memory is definitely when I got a short scholarship for a 3-month course from the government of Ireland 10 years ago. It was fully covered so I practically did not spent any money at all. Moreover, I was even given some allowance in Euro for my trip and stay. What makes it even memorable was that I had a chance to make friends with students from around the world there and got a chance to see the football match live in Aviva Stadium between Republic of Ireland vs Brasil. I also got a signature and picture with my favourite players back then, Robbie Keane and Shay Given plus Ronaldinho. That was the best memory I've ever had.

30. buatlah paragraf tentang animals dengan bahasa inggris


The Lion and the Mouse

One day, a Lion was sleeping in the forest. A little Mouse passed by and saw the Lion. He liked the Lion's mane and started to play with it. The Lion got up and got very angry. He caught the Mouse in his paws and roared loudly. He wanted to eat the Mouse but the Mouse squeaked. He asked the Lion not to eat him. He promised the Lion to help him someday. The Lion laughed but he let the Mouse go.

Some days later, the Lion got caught in a hunter's net. He roared and tried to break free but he couldn't. He roared loudly and asked for help. The Mouse heard the Lion's roar. He come and helped the Lion. He bit the net with his sharp teeth and the Lion was free. He thanked the Mouse for his help. From that day on, the Lion and the Mouse were good friends.
