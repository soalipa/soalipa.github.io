1. contoh menyusun kalimat Acak menjadi teks prosedur


Contoh menyusun kalimat acak menjadi teks prosedur adalah sebagai berikut:


Jumbled sentences:


How to Make an Omelette

(1) Pour the egg mixture into the center of the pan and cook over a high heat for 1-2 minutes

(2) Warm a medium frying pan over a high heat and a dash of oil.

(3) Gently beat the egg together with salt, pepper, and a teaspoon of cold water.

(4) As the egg begins to set, use a spatula to push the set egg towards the omelette center.

(5) Cook the set omelette for another minute, then loosen the edges with a spatula and fold the omelette in half.

(6) Tilt the pan and slide the omelette onto a warm plate and serve immediately on its own or with a crips green salad.

(7) Continue this action until the entire egg mixture is set.


Kalimat di atas tentunya belum memiliki struktur yang tepat. Nah, disini kakak akan menjadikannya sebuah paragraph yang padu (procedural text)


Pembahasan
How to Make an Omelette

Warm a medium frying pan over a high heat, and a dash of oil.

Gently beat the egg together with salt, pepper and a teaspoon of cold water.

Continue this action until the entire egg mixture is set.

Pour the egg mixture into the center of the pan and cook over a high heat for 1-2 minutes.

As the egg begins to set, use a spatula to push the set egg towards the omelette center.

Cook the set omelette for another minute, then loosen the edges with a spatula and fold the omelette in half.

Tilt the pan and slide the omelette onto a warm plate and serve immediately on its own or with a crips green salad.


Sehingga urutan cara pembuatan omelette yang benar sesuai nomer adalah (2-3-7-1-4-5-6)

8. Susunlah kalimat-kalimat acak tersebut menjadi sebuah teks narasi!


Teks narasi  adalah sebuah karya sastra Indonesia yang menyajikan informasi berupa rangkaian peristiwa yang disusun secara kronologis.

Pembahasan

Pada kesempatan ini, soal menyajikan kita dengan beberapa kalimat. Kemudian, ktia diminta untuk menyusun kalimat-kalimat acak tersebut agar menjadi teks narasi yang padu. Berikut kakak akan mencoba menjawab pertanyaan tersebut.
