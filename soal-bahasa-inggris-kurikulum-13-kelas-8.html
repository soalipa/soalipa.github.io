7. jawaban bahasa inggris kelas 9 kurikulum 2013 hal 13 dann 14


Jawaban buku Bahasa Inggris kelas 9 kurikulum 2013 halaman 13 dan 14 adalah tentang melengkapi dialog dengan complimenting and congratulating expression. Untuk melengkap dialog tersebut, kita harus memperhatikan situasi yang diberikan.

Pembahasan

Situation 2:

Lina has just finished drawing a picture of Mrs Ani , her english teacher. She will present it to her on her birthday. Siti praises the picture and she's sure that Mrs. Ani will like it.

Siti: “Lina, that is a beautiful picture. I am sure Mrs. Ani will like it.”

Beni:"Thanks. I hope so too."

Situation 3:

Udin has just finished making fried tofu for his friends. Dayu says that it looks nice and she’s sure that everybody will like it.

Dayu: “Great, your fried tofu looks nice. I am sure everybody will like it.”

Siti: “Thank you. I hope so too.”

Situation 4:

Beni has just finished mopping the floor of the health unit. Mr. Dani praises

him. He also thanks him for that.

Mr. Dani: “Good on you Beni. The floor of the health unit is clean now! Thanks.”

Beni: “You are welcome.”

Situation 5:

Siti has just finished practicing singing. She will sing in the celebration of the Kartini Day. Dayu praises her that she sings like a real singer, and she will get a big applause for that.

Siti: “You sing like a real singer. I’m sure you will get a very big applause.” 

Dayu: “Thank you. That’s very nice of you.”

Jadi, kalimat pertama pada situasi 2, 3 dan 5 adalah kalimat pujian (complimenting expression) yang kemudian diikuti dengan kalimat pengharapan (hoping expression) dengan menggunakan simple future tense (subject + will + verb 1).

Sedangkan pada situasi 4, kalimat pertama adalah complimenting expression yang kemudian diikuti dengan kalimat berterima kasih (thanking expression).

8. buku bahasa inggris halaman 37 kelas 8 kurikulum 2013


"Should" is a modal verb most commonly used to make recommendations or give advice. It can also be used to express obligation as well as expectation.


Examples:

When you go to Berlin, you should visit the palaces in Potsdam. recommendationYou should focus more on your family and less on work. adviceI really should be in the office by 7:00 AM. obligation By now, they should already be in Dubai. expectation
Answering your question:

3. I will go out, but it’s very cloudy now.

You should take an umbrella with you, just in case it will be raining later.


4. I think I’m catching a cold.

You should eat chicken soup and ginger drink. That is usually what I do when I’m catching a cold. That helps a lot.


5. Some students leave litter on their desks.

You should tell the teacher to warn them. I hope they will stop littering on their desks.


6. I sit in the back row. I cannot see you writing on the blackboard.

You should move your seat to the front row, therefor you can see the writing in the blackboard better.


7. I’m longing for my cousins.

You should give her a phone call, or chat. Maybe you could arrange a meeting with her after a while?


8. My legs are asleep because I’m sitting on them.

You should relax your legs and do a little massage to help regulate your blood circulation.


9. Our classroom is dirty and messy.

You should encourage your classmates to clean and organize the class on free day. Because classroom is everybody’s responsibility, not only yours.


10. I have a toothace.

You should visit the dentist soon. Trust me, you don’t want to be late for that. The pain was excruciating for me.


11. I can’t stop yawning.

You should take a break for a while from all the activity that your doing to help yourself relax. Or maybe grab some coffee?


12. I have hiccups.

You should take a long and deep breath, and hold it for a minute. Your hiccups will gone in a second. My mother taught me about this trick, and it’s always working.

29. jawab bahasa Inggris halaman 25 kurikulum 2018 kelas 8


Untuk menyelesaikan tugas ini, kamu harus mengerjakannya bersama dengan anggota kelompokmu. Kamu harus menanyakan anggota kelompokmu mengenai kemampuan apa yang mereka miliki dalam Bahasa Inggris, musik, olahraga, dan pekerjaan rumah. Setelah itu, kamu harus menuliskan data yang kamu peroleh setelah menanyakan anggota kelompokmu ke dalam tabel.

Berikut akan saya berikan contoh pertanyaan yang dapat kamu tanyakan ke teman sekelompokmu dan juga contoh kalimat yang dapat kamu tulis.

Questions

- What can you do in English, music, sports, housework?

- What can you not do in English, music sports, housework?

- Make sentences showing ability using can and can't.

Discussion

In order to ask someone's ability, we usually use the modal verb 'can'. There are two types of questions we can ask.

- Can you + verb + noun ?

For example:

A: Can you speak English well?

B: Yes, I can/No, I can't.

- What can you + verb + noun ?

A: What can you do in English?

B: I can read an English book well.

Answers

Let's take a look at these dialogues. I'll give one dialogue sample to fill in the table.

(2) This is a dialogue between me and my team member, Dina.

Me: Dina, can I ask you some questions about English?

Dina: Yes, sure.

Me: Can you read story in English?

Dina: Yes, I can.

Me: Cool. Next, can you answer questions in English?

Dina: Yes, I can. But I can answer only simple questions.

Me: Can you ask questions in English?

Dina: No, I can't.

Me: I have more questions. Can you play musical instrument?

Dina: Yes, I can.

Me: What can you play?

Dina: I can play piano and sing J-POP songs.

Me: Cool! What sports can you play?

Dina: I can play badminton.

Me: That's all? Can you do housework?

Dina: Yes, I can. I can bake a cake and cook rice.

Me: Last one, can you do other things?

Dina: Let me see. I can paint and write a story.

And then I will put the information I got from Dina to the table.

Pembahasan:

A subject is a part of a sentence that contains the person or thing performing the action (or verb) in a sentence. (See What is a verb?) Here are some examples:

Example : Jennifer walked to the store. In this sentence, the subject is "Jennifer" and the verb is "walked."

Verbs are words that describe an action or talk about something that happens. They take many different forms depending on their subjects, the time they refer to and other ideas we want to express.

Simple past tense adalah kalimat yang mengekspresikan kejadian yang sudah terjadi dimasa lampau. Verb yang digunakan yaitu verb 2. Terdapat 2 bentuk verb yakni:

Irregular (tidak beraturan) = Awake - Awoke

Regular (beraturan) = tambahkan -ed/-d di akhir kata kerja. = Stay - Stayed.
