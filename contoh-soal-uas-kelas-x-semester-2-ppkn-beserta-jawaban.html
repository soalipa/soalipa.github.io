-to my mind....

Difa: In my opinion, my school is great. The facilities are good and there is a large school field there.

Difa: My class is good. My teacher said that my class is the cleanest class in the school.
Linda: Wow... You have great school and class
Difa: Yes, you right. Sometimes, you must visit my school.

Questions :
1. What is the dialog about?
2. Who is asking opinion and who is giving opinion?
3. What is Difa's opinion about her school?
4. What is Difa's opinion about her class?

3. In Difa's opinion, her school is great. The facilities is good and the school have a large field.
