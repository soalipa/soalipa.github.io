Once when a lion was asleep, a little mouse began running up and down upon him; this soon awoke the lion, who placed his huge paw upon him, and open his big jaws to swallow him.

“Pardon, O king,” cried the little mouse, “Forgive me this time, I shall never forget it; perhaps I may be able to do you a good turn some of these days.”

The lion was so tickled at the idea of the mouse being able to help him. Then, he lifted up his paw and let him go.

Sometime after, the lion was caught in a trap. Some hunters, who wanted to carry him alive to the King, tied him to a tree while they went in search of a wagon to carry him in. No.4. Sistim Persamaan Linear dan Kuadrat

Substitusikan y = x + 4 ke persamaan kedua
x² + (x + 4)² = 16
x² + x² + 8x + 16 = 16
2x² + 8x = 0
x² + 4x = 0
x(x + 4) = 0
Diperoleh x = 0 atau x = -4

No.16 Turunan Trigonometri

y = (sin 2x)(cos x)
y' = u'v + uv'

y' = (2cos 2x)(cos x) + (sin 2x)(-sin x)
y' = (2(2cos²x - 1))(cos x) - (2sin x.cos x)(sin x)

28. berikan jawaban latihan soal pat /ukk kelas 8 Bahasa Inggris beri kunci jawaban nya​


Soal 1:

Choose the correct form of the verb to complete the sentence:

My sister ________ tennis every Saturday morning.

a) plays

b) play

c) played

d) playing

Jawaban: a) plays

Soal 2:

Complete the sentence with the correct preposition:

I'm going to the store ________ some groceries.

a) at

b) with

c) for

d) to

Jawaban: c) for

Soal 3:

Choose the correct question tag for the following sentence:

He doesn't like coffee, ________?

a) does he?

b) isn't he?

c) doesn't he?

d) don't he?

Jawaban: a) does he?

Soal 4:

Identify the correct comparative form of the adjective "good":

a) gooder

b) goodest

c) better

d) goodier

Jawaban: c) better

Soal 5:

Choose the correct form of the verb to complete the sentence:

She ________ a book when I arrived.

a) reads

b) is reading

c) read

d) has read

Jawaban: b) is reading

Soal 6:

Choose the correct word order for the sentence:

carrots / do / like / you / ?

a) You do like carrots?

b) Do you like carrots?

c) Carrots you do like?

d) Do like you carrots?

Jawaban: b) Do you like carrots?

Soal 7:

Identify the correct spelling of the word:

a) beutiful

b) beutifull

c) beautiful

d) beautifull

Jawaban: c) beautiful

Soal 8:

Choose the correct form of the verb to complete the sentence:

The students ________ to the teacher's instructions.

a) listens

b) listen

c) listened

d) listening

Jawaban: b) listen

Soal 9:

Choose the correct possessive form of the noun:

The dog is ________ bone.

a) its

b) it's

c) their

d) they're

Jawaban: a) its

Soal 10:

Identify the correct punctuation for the sentence:

My favorite color is blue ________.

a) .

b) ,

c) !

d) ?

Jawaban: a) .

Kode : 11.2.3 (Kelas 11 Matematika Bab 3 - Trigonometri Lanjut), 11.2.7 (Kelas 11 Matematika Bab 7 - Limit)

Pembahasan :

1) cos 2x - 3 cos x + 2 = 0
=> (2 cos² x - 1) - 3 cos x + 2 = 0
Misal cos x = a
=> (2a² - 1) - 3a + 2 = 0
=> 2a² - 3a + 1 = 0
=> (2a - 1)(a - 1) = 0
=> a = 1/2 atau a = 1

Untuk a = 1/2
cos x = 1/2
Kuadran 1 : x = 60° = π/3
Kuadran 4 : x = 300° = (5/3) π

Untuk a = 1
cos x = 1
x = 0° = 0
x = 360° = 2π

HP = {0, π/3, (5/3) π, 2π}


2) Rumus yang digunakan :
Lim √(ax² + bx + c) - √(px² + qx + r)
x=>~
Jika a > p => ~
Jika a = p => (b - q)/(2√a)
Jika a < p => -~

Lim √(4x² + 6x - 7) - 2x - 3
x=>~

Lim √(4x² + 6x - 7) - (2x + 3)
x=>~

Lim √(4x² + 6x - 7) - √(2x + 3)²
x=>~

Lim √(4x² + 6x - 7) - √(4x² + 12x + 9)
x=>~

a = 4, b = 6, c = -7, p = 4, q = 12, r = 9
Karena a = p maka bisa menggunakan rumus :

(b - q)/(2√a)
= (6 - 12)/(2√4)
= -6/4
= -3/2
= -1 1/2
= -1,5
