Percakapan Bahasa Inggris: Berkenalan dengan Orang Lain (Greeting & Introduction)

Get acquainted with others

Mengenalkan diri merupakan sebuah kegiatan yang paling umum dilakukan, terlebih ketika seseorang berharap ingin membangun hubungan dengan orang lain, tidak terkecuali macam hubungan yang sedang dijalin. Dengan berkenalan terlebih dahulu, selanjutnya akan terjadi percakapan dan hubungan yang lebih baik.

Perkenalan juga dapat dibedakan baik secara formal maupun secara informal. Adapun perkenalan secara formal misalnya perkenalan dengan teman kerja atau dengan partner bisnis dan segala perkenalan yang membutuhkan kata kata formal.

Berikut adalah beberapa contoh yang dapat digunakan untuk sebuah perkenalan formal.

Hi! My name is Eadlyn.

(Hai, nama saya adalah Eadlyn.)

Good morning/afternoon/evening. I am Eadlyn. Nice to meet you.

(Selamat pagi/siang/malam. Saya Eadlyn. Senang bertemu dengan Anda.)

How are you today? My name is Eadlyn!

(Apa kabar Anda hari ini? Nama saya Eadlyn.)

I am Eadlyn. It is very nice to meet you.

(Nama saya Eadlyn/Saya Eadlyn. Senang sekali bertemu dengan Anda.)

Hello! I am Eadlyn. I am happy to finally meet you.

(Halo! Saya Eadlyn. Saya senang akhirnya dapat bertemu dengan Anda.)

Hi, good morning. My name is Eadlyn. I come from Bandung.

(Hai, selamat pagi. Nama saya Eadlyn. Saya datang dari Bandung.)

26. TEKS BERITA BAHASA INGGRIS HARI INI 21 NOV. 2022, TOLONG ISI SEKALIAN TRANSLATE NYA SOALNYA MAU PRAKTEK​


Teks Berita Bahasa Inggris hari ini 21 November 2022 tentang World Children's Day as momentum to fulfill basic immunization rights :

The Coordinating Ministry for Human Development and Culture emphasizes that the commemoration of World Children's Day which falls on November 20 every year, must become a reminder of the fulfillment of basic immunization rights for children.

"World Children's Day is a momentum to fulfill children's rights to basic immunization," the ministry's deputy for coordinating health quality improvement and population development, Agus Suprapto, said here, Sunday.

He added that basic immunization was important to provide optimal medical protection for Indonesian children.

"Basic immunization is very useful to protect children from various diseases that can be prevented by vaccination, for example, polio, diphtheria, measles, rubella, hepatitis B, and various other diseases," Suprapto added

Basic immunization can also support children's growth and development to become more optimal, considering that children are the next generation of the nation, he remarked.

"As the nation's next generation and future leaders, all parties must ensure that our children are able to grow and develop optimally," he conveyed.

He noted that the COVID-19 pandemic was feared to affect the coverage of basic immunization in the country.

Thus the commemoration of World Children's Day can be the right momentum to pursue the target of basic immunization for children and increase the coverage of basic immunization for children in the country," he said.

Suprapto also stated that the government continued to intensify education and socialization to the public regarding the importance of basic immunization.

"The government has also created a National Child Immunization Month (BIAN) and School Children Immunization Month program to support the acceleration in increasing basic immunization coverage," he disclosed.

With the common awareness of the whole community to complete basic immunization for children, it is hoped that herd immunity will be achieved according to the target.

"The ministry invites the whole community to protect Indonesian children together through immunization. Please come to the community health center (puskesmas) immediately to get basic immunizations such as polio, DPT-HB-Hib, PCV, and measles-rubella," he said.

39. kisi kisi bahasa indonesia kelas 6 materi pidhato ujian praktek​


Jawaban:

KOMPETENSI DASAR

:

Berbicara, mengungkapkan pikiran, perasaan, dan informasi dalam bentuk karangan sederhana, petunjuk, surat, pengumuman, dialog, formulir, teks pidato, laporan, ringkasan, prafrase, serta berbagai karya sastra untuk anak berbentuk cerita, puisi, dan pantun.

MATERI

:

Pidato

INDIKATOR

:

Siswa mampu berpidato untuk berbagai keperluan (Perayaan HUT RI, Peringatah Hari Pahlawan dan Perayaan Hari Guru). Dengan lafal, intonasi, dan sikap yang tepat.

Petunjuk pengerjaan :

Siswa menyusun sebuah tek pidato dengan memilih salah satu dari tema yang telah disediakan diantaranya (Perayaan HUT RI, Peringatah Hari Pahlawan dan Perayaan Hari Guru)

Teks pidato disusun minimal 4 (empat) paragraph dengan susunan : paragraph 1 berupa pembukaan, paragraph 2 dan 3 berupa isi pidato dan paragraph 4 merupakan pidato penutup).

Gunakan bahasa yang baik dan benar sesuai dengan Ejaan Yang Disempurnakan (EYD)

Bacakan teks pidatomu di depan kelas dengan lantang pada saat pelaksanaan ujian praktek Pelajaran Bahasa Indonesia pada hari Senin, 25 Februari 2019 sesuai jadwal ujian praktek yang telah diinformasikan.

Ktriteria Penilaian Praktek Pidato
