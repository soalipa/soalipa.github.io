7. apa rumus simple present tense dan continous tense ?


simple present tense:
verbal:
s (she/he/it) +verb 1 with s/es
s (she/he/it) + does not + verb1
does + s (she/he/it) = verb1?

s (i/you/they/we) + verb 1
 s (i/you/they/we) + do not +verb 1
 do + s (i/you/they/we) + verb 1
 
nominal:
s + am/is/are + noun
s+ am/is/are + not + noun
is/are + s + noun?

present continuous tense:
s + am/is/are + verb-ing + o
 s + am/is/are + not + verb-ing + o
is/are + s + verb-ing + o?

Kalimat Active :

1. Simple Present : He reads the book in library.

2. Simple past : He read the book in library.

3. Simple future : He will read the book in library.

4. Present Continuous : He is reading the book in library.

5. Past Continuous : He was reading the book in library.

6. Present Perfect : He has read the book in library.

7. Past Perfect : He had read the book in library.

Kalimat Passive :

1. Simple Present : The book is read by him in library.

2. Simple past : The book was read by him in library.

3. Simple future : The book will be read by him in library.

4. Present continuous : The book is being read by him in library.

5. Past continuous : The book was being read by him in library

6. present perfect : The book has been read by him in library.

7. past perfect : The book had been read by him in Library

15. Deskripsi tentang -simple present tense -present continous tense -past tense


Description about :

___________________

SIMPLE PRESENT TENSE

Jenis tenses ini menjabarkan tentang fakta kebenaran ( General truth ), dan suatu kegiatan yang dilakukan berulang-ulang ( kebiasaan )Menggunakan ciri khas keterangan frekuensi ( adverb of frequency ) , seperti :usually, often, everyday, seldom, rarely, never, sometimes, always Menggunakan rumus , sbb :Subject+verb(s/es) + O/C  ( kalimat verbal )Subject + tobe (is/am/are) + noun/adjective/adverb   ( kalimat nominal )

Example :

This earth is round    ( Bumi ini bulat ==> Fakta / fenomena alam )

Our President is Mr. Jokowi   ( Presiden kita adlah Pak Jokowi ==> Fakta)

She often helps her mother ( Kebiasaan )

PRESENT CONTINUOUS TENSE

Jenis tenses ini menjabarkan perihal kegiatan yang sedang dilakukan ketika dibicarakan sesaat.Menggunakan ciri khas adverbs of time :right now, now, at the moment, stillMenggunakan rumus, sbb :Subject+ tobe(is/am/are) + verb-ing + O/Complement

Example  :

They are watching TV right now

She is studying English in her room at the moment

SIMPLE PAST TENSE

Tenses ini menjabarkan perihal kegiatan/aktivitas/kejadian yang sudah terjadi di waktu lampau atau waktu yang spesifik (tertentu).Penggunaan adverbs of time bentukan lampau , sbb :yesterday, last month, 2 days ago, in 1889Menggunakan rumus , sbb :Subject+verb 2 + O / Complement    ( kalimat verbal )Subject+tobe ( was/were) + noun/adjective+adverb   ( kalimat nominal )

Example :

Roy went to Bali two weeks ago.

She was sick yesterday.

France built the Eiffel tower in 1887

18. Cerita bahasa inggris dengan kata kerja simple present, simple past, present continous


Recount Text

Recount Text adalah teks yang bertujuan untuk menceritakan pengalaman atau kejadian yang dialami di masa lampau.  

Generic Structure dari Recount text adalah:

1. Orientation: Berisi pengenalan tokoh, tempat, waktu dan tujuan cerita.

2. Events: Berisi hal-hal yang terjadi pada waktu itu (lampau).

3. Re-orientation: Berisi kesimpulan cerita atau kesan dari penulis/ tokoh terhadap pengalaman/ ceritanya.

Language features dari Recount Text:

1. Menggunakan Past tense

2. Menggunakan action verb

3. Menggunakan thinking verb.

Pembahasan

Cerita bahasa inggris dengan kata kerja simple present, simple past, present continous adalah:

This is my experience in middle school, exactly 3 years ago when I was in 7th grade. Every year, our school held scout activities which must be followed by all 7th grade students and at the end of each semester there were camp activities held at the school.

When the D-day arrived, before the activity began the opening ceremony was held to get information and direction during the camp activities. After that, my team mate and I immediately set up a tent that was assisted by a coach. After the tent was established, we immediately arranged the equipment we had brought. Shortly, the whistle rang, I quickly put on a tie and other scout gear because there would be a game between teams to train the cohesiveness and togetherness of each team. Then there was heavy rain. Considering that all the equipment was in the tent, the school finally decided that all students would take refuge in the hall, let the conditions where it was not possible to come in because there were several tents which had collapsed by the wind, and the field were very muddy. Arriving at the hall, we immediately rushed to the bathroom to change our clothes.  

After each team gathered according to each class, everyone got a box of rice which could be enjoyed together. After dinner, the rain stopped. We went straight to the courtyard to enjoy the campfire and watch the performances of each team, in the form of songs and poems. After the campfire event was over, the school announced that the female students in that night must sleep in the meeting room, while the men remained in the hall. Finally we were moved again. In our hearts, we were very happy because of the meeting room has an air conditioner, and it might not be fair for men to have to sleep without air conditioner.

The next morning at around 5 a.m, all students had to get up and get ready, because morning exercise would be held. After that, we enjoyed the breakfast together. We didn't forget to clear the tent that had collapsed and saved it again. Around 8 o'clock, we prepared for the closing ceremony. This was an unforgettable experience.

20. Pola kalimat simple present tense dan simple continous tense


Pola kalimat Simple Present Tense terbagi menjadi 3, yaitu pola kalimat positif, negatif, dan tanya. Jika objek dalam kalimat merupakan objek tunggal (singular subject), bisa membuat kalimat Simple Present Tense dengan rumus S + Verb 1(s/es). Di belakang kata kerjanya ditambahkan akhiran -s atau -es.

Present continuous tense dibentuk menggunakan subjek + kata kerja bantu (auxiliary verb) yaitu be dalam bentuk am/are/is + Verb-ing + complement atau pelengkap.

Kalau Pasangan "to be" nya:

I = Am

She = Is

He = Is

It = Is

We = Are

They = Are

Lebih dari satu orang = Are

I and my friends = Are

Kalau Verb atau Predikat atau Kata Kerjanya untuk present continuous:

Note: Continuous maksudnya adalah kegiatannya sedang dilakukan.

Ini tinggal tambahin "ing" di belakang kata kerjanya:

ngomong = lagi + ngomong = lagi ngomong-ngomong

speak      =   speak + ing      = speaking

nyanyi = lagi + nyanyi = lagi menyanyi

sing    = sing + ing   = singing

nari       = lagi + nari = lagi menari

dance = dance + ing = dancing

note: ada aturan; bila kata kerja diakhiri huruf "e" maka huruf "e"nya dimusnahkan saja, dan tinggal tambahin "ing" di belakannya (eg: "fire" menjadi "firing", "bake" menjadi "baking")

pengecualian beberapa kata irregular kayak:

mati = lagi + mati = lagi dalam proses mati

die = die + ing = dying - ini dia malah ganti "ie" pada "die" dengan "y"

tinta = lagi + tinta = lagi meninta (mewarnai dengan tinta)

dye = dye + ing = dyeing - ini huruf "e" pada "dye" malah gk dihilangin dan langsung ditambahin "ing", hanya supaya katanya gk jadi sama dengan kata irregular "dying" yang di atas tadi.

Kemudian ada lagi aturan, khusus kata kerja yang huruf belakannya "c"

panik = lagi + panik = lagi panik

panic = panic + ing = panicking

Jadi, perlu ditambahin huruf "k" sebelum "ing" itu.

Dan kemudian ada lagi aturan; Verbs ending with a single vowel plus a consonant.. Ini agak susah aku jelasin sekarang, karena dalam aturan ini, ada banyak variabel yang perlu diperhatikan.

Jawaban Soal:

Maka, untuk jawab soal itu, kita hanya perlu tambahin "to be" dan "verb+ing".

Seperti:

I _____ (fly) in the sky

dijawab:

I am flying in the sky - "am" karena itu to be nya "I", dan "flying" karena "fly+ing"

She _____ (jump) using a trampoline

She is jumping using a trampoline - "is" karena itu to be nya "She", dan "jumping" karena "jump + ing"

Aku lihat semua soal itu mengikuti aturan simple ini aja kok; to be + verb(ing). Jadi "note" aku yang di atas tidak dibutuhkan untuk menjawab soal ini.

36. tolong buatkan cerita bahasa inggris yang mengandung simple future tense,simple past future tense,dan present continous tense


Explanation:

- Simple future tenseThis tense is used to tell about an action that will be happened in the future.The structure: S + will + V1 + O
For example, I will go to Bali next month.

- Simple past future tense
This tense is used to indicate an action which would be happened in the past. This tense is mostly used to show wish or hope.
The structure: S + would + V1 + O
For example, I wish he would forgive me.

- Present continuous tenseThis tense is used to tell about something that is in progress right now.
The structure: S + to be (am / is / are) + V-ing + O
For example, She is reading a book in the library.
