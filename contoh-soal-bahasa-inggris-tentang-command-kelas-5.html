example (contoh) : (aku kasih lebih ya)

1. Please, have a seat!              (Silahkan duduk!)

5. Please, come to my house    (Mohon datang ke rumah ku!)
6. Please, stop by my office!      (Mohon mampirlah ke kantorku!)
7. Please, save me!                   (Tolong selamatkan aku!)                              8. Please, pay attention!            (Mohon perhatiannya!)
9. Please, send me the letter!    (Tolong, kirimkan aku surat!)
10. Please, don’t cry!                 (Mohon janagan menangis!)
