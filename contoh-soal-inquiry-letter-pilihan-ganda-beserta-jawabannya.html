Jawaban:

Apt BLK 30, Eunos St. #05 – 116

Singapore 1441

2nd April 2009

Dear Liza,

I have received you letter asking me to meet you at your house this Sunday to discuss the urgent matter you have. I regret however to say that it will not be possible for me to see you on Sunday as I already have a previous appointment . I shall see you on Monday next week at 5 p.m . Your sincerely

Daisy

A. Liza’s problem

B. Liza’s appointment

C. Daisy’s previous letter to Liza

D. Daisy’s regret for not being able to come

16. soal personal letter beserta jawabannya


Q : what is the structure of personal letter?
A  : 1. Date : Date when the letter is writen (top left).
      2. Address : Place where you are writing fron (top right).
      3. Salutation & Name : Greeting and the person’s name you are writing to.
      4. Introduction : The opening of the letter usually starts with “how are you?”  or refers to previous letter.
      5. Body : The main part of the letter. It includes what you want to write to the other person.
      6. Closure : The part indicates the letter is going to end.
      7. Complimentary close : Short expressions like “love you” , “sincerely yours” , “love”.
      8. Signature : Signature or initials of the writer.
      9. Postscript P.S : After thougt in a letter. You begin with P.S. and end it with your initials.

21. PILIHAN GANDA Keluaran dari program berikut adalah ? for letter in 'hello': if letter == 'e' or letter == 's': break print(letter) a.se b.es c.s d.e e.hello Ada yang tau jawabannya apa?


Jawaban:

Jawabannya D.E

Penjelasan:

Program di atas akan mencetak huruf-huruf dalam kata "hello" satu per satu. Namun, ketika program mencapai huruf 'e', kondisi `if letter == 'e' or letter == 's':` akan dievaluasi. Karena 'e' terpenuhi sebagai huruf yang sama dengan 'e', program akan keluar dari loop `for` menggunakan pernyataan `break`.

Akibatnya, program hanya akan mencetak huruf 'h' dan 'e' sebelum keluar dari loop. Sebagai hasilnya, output yang dihasilkan adalah "he" dan bukan "hello" secara keseluruhan. Oleh karena itu, jawaban yang benar adalah d. e.

Dear Mr.Villalibre,

Ahmed Al-Hilal

He knows it from Instagram.

3. When is the suggested online meeting expected to be held? This week.

It means to 'hold' or 'do'.
