1. What is self introduction?

A self-introduction explains who you are, what you do and what others need to know about you. You should provide a self-introduction any time you meet someone new and don't have a third party to introduce you. Offer a self-introduction when you are: Beginning an interview.

Atau simplenya introduction bisa kita artikan sebagai "Perkenalan".

2. What are the expressions of self introduction (formal)?

- I would like to introduce myself. My name is…

- May I introduce myself. My name is…

- Let me introduce myself. My name is…

- Would you mind if I introduce myself. My name is…

Perkenalan (introducing) formal ini biasanya digunakan pada acara atau forum resmi, seperti perkenalan pada acara seminar dimana diacara tersebut terdapat tokoh atau orang penting

3. What are the expressions of self introduction (informal)?

- Hi, Good morning. I’m Shasya from Jakarta

- Hey bro/sis, how do you do? I’m Aulia, nice to see you.

Perkenalan (introducing) formal ini biasanya digunakan pada acara atau forum tidak resmi, seperti perkenalan pada saat ditempat umum bertemu dengan saudara teman atau teman baru. 

4. What are the responses of self introduction (formal)?

- I am very pleased to meet you.

- Glad to meet you too.

5. What are the responses of self introduction (informal)?

- I'm so happy to talk with you

- Lovely to meet you too

Jawaban:

1. Self introduction can help us in various situations, such as during job interviews, networking events, social gatherings, or when meeting new people. It allows us to provide a brief overview of ourselves, our background, skills, and interests, helping others to get to know us better.

2. A strong self introduction can make a positive impression on others and create a lasting impact. It can help us stand out from the crowd, showcase our confidence, and effectively communicate our strengths and abilities. A strong self introduction can also help in building professional connections, establishing rapport, and opening up opportunities for further conversations or collaborations.

3. Self introduction initiates the process of introducing ourselves to others in a formal or informal setting. It serves as a starting point for communication, setting the tone for further interactions. Self introduction allows others to gain initial insights into who we are, what we do, and what we can offer, facilitating better understanding and engagement.

4. Self introduction benefits us in school by helping us introduce ourselves to classmates, teachers, and staff members. It promotes a positive classroom environment, encourages interaction, and fosters relationships with peers. Self introduction can also enhance our communication skills, boost our confidence, and create opportunities for collaboration and participation in various school activities or projects. Additionally, it allows teachers and classmates to learn about our interests, strengths, and goals, which can facilitate better support and engagement within the academic community.

21. Materi Self introduction


Jawaban:

Self-introduction atau perkenalan diri adalah cara untuk memperkenalkan diri kita sendiri kepada orang lain. Berikut adalah beberapa hal yang dapat disertakan dalam self-introduction:

1. Nama: Mulailah dengan memperkenalkan diri dengan nama lengkap.

2. Asal: Sebutkan asal daerah atau kota tempat tinggal.

3. Pendidikan: Sebutkan pendidikan terakhir yang telah dicapai.

4. Pengalaman: Jika ada, sebutkan pengalaman kerja atau organisasi yang pernah diikuti.

5. Hobi: Sebutkan hobi atau kegiatan favorit yang sering dilakukan.

6. Kepribadian: Jelaskan karakteristik atau kepribadian yang dimiliki.

Contoh self-introduction:

Halo, perkenalkan nama saya Andi Pratama. Saya berasal dari Bandung dan telah menyelesaikan pendidikan S1 di Universitas Padjajaran. Saya memiliki pengalaman kerja sebagai marketing di sebuah perusahaan retail selama 3 tahun. Hobi saya adalah bermain badminton dan membaca buku. Saya dikenal sebagai orang yang pekerja keras dan rajin dalam menyelesaikan tugas. Senang berkenalan dengan kalian semua.
