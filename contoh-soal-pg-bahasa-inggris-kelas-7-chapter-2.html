22. Contoh soal pg bahasa inggris tentang congratulation, hope dan wish​


Jawaban:

Ranti: "Congratulations Salsa! you've won the short story writing competition. "

Salsa: "Thanks you Ranti. I'm really happy."

Ranti "You're welcome, I hope I can also participate in this competition next year"

Salsa: "of course,"

Ranti: "I wish actually enter this competition. "

maaf kalo salah

Jawaban:

what you say to your friend if won a competition?

a. congratulation

b. i wish

c. i hope

what did you say if your mom birthday?

a. i hope you have a long life

b. congratulation on your birthday

c. i wish you have a long life

What did you say for your best friend failed

a. i wish you success

b. i hope you success

c. congratulation

The answer:

1. a

2.c

3.b

Penjelasan:

congratulation: dipakai saat mengucapkan selamat atas keberhasilan

Wish: dipakai saat pembicaraan yang tidak kemungkinan bisa tercapai contoh i wish i can fly

Hope: dipakai saat pembicaraan yang kemungkinan bisa tercapai dan masih bisa terjadi contoh I wish I could do the test

Semoga membantu dan mohon maaf jika salah

1. **Vocabulary:**

What is the meaning of the word "benevolent" as used in Chapter 6? Provide a brief definition.

2. **Comprehension:**

Summarize the main events that took place in Chapter 6. Include at least three key points.

3. **Grammar:**

Identify the verb tense used in the following sentence from Chapter 6: "She had been waiting for hours."

4. **Inference:**

Based on the information in Chapter 6, why do you think the main character made a particular decision? Provide evidence from the text to support your answer.

5. **Creative Writing:**

Imagine you are one of the characters in Chapter 6. Write a diary entry expressing your thoughts and feelings about the events that occurred. Be sure to use appropriate vocabulary and verb tenses.
