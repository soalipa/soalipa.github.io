16. Contoh dialog bahasa Inggris tentang opinion 7 orang yg singkat


Contoh Dialog Asking and Giving Opinion – 1

Situation: Trevor and Matt are discussing issues of banning smartphone in the school.

Trevor: Have you heard about our school’s plan to ban smartphones in the school?

Matt : Yeah

Trevor: What do you think about it?

Matt : Well, I think that’s fair enough.

Trevor: why do you think so?

Matt : you see… Smartphones have caused so much trouble in our class. Remember yesterday’s history class? Our teacher got very angry because was interrupt by smartphone ringing many times. This may happen again and again.

Trevor: Eh… Yea…

Matt : Another thing is… Smartphones have caused a kind of stupid competition.

Trevor: what do you mean?

Matt : you know, Everyone seems to compete to have the latest model.

Trevor: Yes, I feel that too.

Matt : So it’s a good idea if the school doesn’t allow students to use their smartphone in the school.

Contoh Dialog Asking and Giving Opinion – 2

Situation: Vidia and Ryan are arguing about smoking.

Vidia: Hi Ryan, by the way, I just don’t understand why you guys like smoking?

Ryan : Well… It’s nice you know…

Vidia: Nice? What’s nice about smoking? In my opinion, there is nothing nice about smoking; it’s dangerous for your health. And it is waste of money

Ryan : I’ve heard many times, but I don’t believe it.

Vidia: Maybe you’ll believe it when you get your lung cancer.

Contoh Dialog Asking and Giving Opinion – 3

Situation: Maggie and Tina discussing global warming

Maggie: Tina, what do you think is the best action to reduce global warming?

Tina : I think everyone should start changing their life styles.

Maggie: What do you mean?

Tina : Well, we have to start to do what we can, to help reduce global warming.

Maggie: What do you suggest that we should do?

Tina : Well, there are lots of things that we can do. We should start saving electricity, recycling things, using public transportation, buying and consuming as much as we need only. Basically, just save anything that we can.

Maggie: that’s a great idea. I will do that.

Contoh Dialog Asking and Giving Opinion – 4

Situation: choosing a dress in a department store.

Naima: I love these two dresses. But I have to choose one. What do you think? Which one is suited to me?

Naomi: I think the dress which has peach color is suited to you.

21. contoh dialog bahasa Inggris giving opinion 2 orang dengan tema liburan ke pantai dalam bahasa Inggris pliss jawab cepet​


Jawaban:

Dialog 1

A: Hey, how are you today? (Hai, bagaimana kabar Anda hari ini?)

B: I’m great thanks. How are you? (Saya baik-baik saja, terima kasih. Bagaimana kabar Anda?)

A: I’m great too, thank you. Aren’t the weather really good today? (Saya baik-baik saja juga, terima kasih. Bukankah cuacanya sangat bagus hari ini?)

B: Very! I was really looking forward to going to this beach since last week and I’m glad the sun is shining so brightly today. (Sangat! Saya sangat menantikan pergi ke pantai ini sejak minggu lalu dan saya senang mataharinya bersinar begitu terang hari ini)

A: Yeah, I was pretty surprised too. I almost cancelled my plan to come here because the weather has been raining a lot lately. (Iya, saya juga cukup terkejut. Saya hampir membatalkan rencana saya untuk pergi ke sini karena cuacanya sering hujan akhir-akhir ini)

B: I’m glad it’s not raining today. (Saya senang hari ini tidak hujan)

A: Me too (Saya juga)

Dialog 2

A: This is such a beautiful beach! Look at these white sands. I love it so much here. Thank you for bringing me here. (Ini pantai yang sangat indah! Lihatlah pantai putih ini. Saya suka sekali di sini. Terima kasih telah membawa saya ke sini)

B: No problem. I found this beach on the internet. There were so many people recommended it because of the beautiful clean sand it has. (Tidak masalah. Saya menemukan pantai ini di internet. Ada begitu banyak orang yang merekomendasikannya karena pantai bersih yang indah yang ada di sini)

A: Moreover, there is not much people here. I think this beach is pretty secluded and only a few people knows this. We are pretty lucky to have found this then. (Ditambah lagi, tidak ada terlalu banyak orang di sini. Sepertinya pantai ini cukup terpencil dan hanya beberapa orang yang mengetahuinya. Kita cukup beruntung karena telah menemukan pantai ini kalau begitu)

B: Very. When I look it up on the map, it was pretty hard to figure out how to get down here. Thank God there was a local man directing us from the main road. (Sangat. Ketika saya mencarinya di peta, cukup sulit untuk mencari tahu bagaimana cara turun ke sini. Untung saja ada orang lokal yang mengarahkan kita dari jalan raya)

A: Enough chit chat now. Let’s go swimming, the waves is really nice and calm! (Cukup mengobrol untuk sekarang. Mari kita berenang, ombaknya sangat bagus dan tenang!)
