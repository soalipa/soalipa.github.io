1. Minta bantuannya tugas soal Bahasa inggris : Simple Sentence, Compound Sentence, Complex Sentence, Compound-Complex Sentence. Kalimatnya harus sama


Jawaban:

Simple sentence: I sleep.

Compound sentence: I am sleepy, so I sleep.

Complex sentence: Because I am sleepy, I sleep.

Compund-complex: Because I am sleepy, I sleep, and it's 11 PM.

Penjelasan:

Independent clause(Main clause): Kalimat yang bisa berdiri sendiri. Contoh: She buys a car. [S + V1 + O]

Dependent clause: Kalimat yang tidak bisa berdiri sendiri. Biasanya diikuti oleh conjunction. Contoh: Because he was hungry. [Because » conjunction, he was hungry (S + to be + C) ].

Simple sentences: Terdiri dari 1 independent clause. Contoh: I play the ball. [S + V1 + O]Compound sentences: Terdiri dari 2 independent clause, dan coordinating conjunction (for, but, and, so, yet). Contoh: I am hungry, but I don't have time to eat. [I am hungry » independent clause (S + to be + C), but » coordinating conjunction, I don't have time to eat » independent clause (S + V) Complex sentences: Terdiri dari 1 independent clause dan minimum 1 dependent clause. Contoh: While I was walking, I saw a great view. [While I was walking » dependent clause, I saw a great view » independent clause]Compound-complex sentences: Terdiri dari compound sentence dan complex sentence. Contoh: I love eating, but when I am full, I don't want to eat. [I love eating » independent clause 1, but » coordinating conjunction, when I am full » dependent clause, I don't want to eat » independent clause.] Sehingga: [I love eating, but » compound sentence, when I am full, I don't want to eat » complex sentence]

3. 10 contoh paragraf complex sentence bahasa inggris


1. After a couple of years, he still remember his ex-girl. [Setelah dua tahun, dia masih ingat mantan pacarnya.]

2. After Anita closed the door, she decided to stop learning  for a while. [Setelah Anita menutup pintu tersebut, ia memutuskan untuk berhenti belajar beberapa saat.]

3. Although I have been invited, I decide not to go. [Meskipun Aku sudah di undang, Aku memutuskan untuk tidak berangkat.]

4. Although Mrs. Satomoto is not a young anymore, she is still very passionate and energetic. [Walaupun ibu Sotomoto usianya sudah tidak muda lagi, ia masih sangat bersemangat dan enerjik.]

5. Anita likes the person who was nice to her. [Anita suka dengan orang yang bersikap baik kepada nya.]

6. Barcelona won the UEFA Champion League in 2015. It was the year when I got married. [Barcelona memenangkan UEFA Champion League tahun 2015. Itu adalah tahun ketika aku menikah.]

7. Because Anita is so beautiful, Joni likes her. [Karena Anita begitu cantik, Joni menyukainya.]

8. Because his parents are so rich, Anton never worry about his future. [Karena ayahnya sangat kaya, Anton tidak pernah khawatir akan masa depan nya.]

9. Because My girlfriend did not bring her wallet, I had to call her to come back home. [Karena pacarku tidak membawa dompetnya, aku harus meneleponnya untuk kembali lagi kerumah.]

10. Before it’s too late, it is better to start it up right now. [Sebelum terlambat, sebaiknya memulai sekarang.]

11. David and Peter finally met Cristiano Ronaldo, whom they had always admired. [David dan Peter akhirnya bertemu dengna Cristiano Ronaldo, orang yang selalu mereka kagumi.]

12. Deadpool was still an enjoyable movie even though it was so long. [Deadpool adalah sebuah film yang masih tetap menyenangkan, meskipun durasinya begitu lama.]

13. Everyone looked at me when I laugh so loud. [Semua orang melihat ke arahku ketika aku tertawa terbahak-bahak.]

14. Have you known those people whom we met at the movie? [Sudahkan kamu mengenal dengan orang-orang yang kita jumpai di bioskop itu?]

15. I didn’t like that song although the singer is Michael Jackson. [Aku tidak menyukai lagu itu meskipun penyanyinya adalah Michael Jackson.]

Adverb Clause Complex Sentences:

1) Although he was tired, he stayed up late to finish his work.2) Because the weather was so bad, we decided to stay inside.3) If she finishes her project on time, she will be able to go on vacation.4) As the sun set, the sky turned a beautiful shade of pink and orange.5) Since he had been studying for so long, he deserved a break.6) Even though the movie was not good, they decided to watch it until the end.7) When the phone rang, he answered it immediately.8) While he was cooking dinner, the kitchen filled with the smell of spices.9) Until the storm passes, we will have to stay inside.10) Unless he studies hard, he will fail the exam.

Adjective Clause Complex Sentences:

1) The book, which was recommended by my friend, was a bestseller.2) The students who arrived early got the best seats.3) The man, whose car broke down on the way, was late for the meeting.4) The dress that she wore to the party was stunning.5) The food, which was cooked by the chef, was delicious.

Noun Clause Complex Sentences:

1) She said that she would be home soon.2) I wonder when he will arrive.3) They asked me what my name was.4) I know that he is right.5) It is clear that the decision was a mistake.

Penjelasan:

Complex Sentence atau Kalimat Kompleks, adalah kalimat dengan satu Independent Clause atau setidaknya lebih dari satu Dependent Clause.Dependent Clause atau Subordinate Clause, adalah klausa yang tidak bisa berdiri sendiri untuk membentuk sebuah kalimat.
Contoh: "Ketika aku tumbuh", (Ketika) memerlukan konteks lainnya.Independent Clause adalah klausa yang memang sudah bisa dikatakan sebuah kalimat.
Contoh: "Aku tumbuh untuk menjadi seorang polisi"

Penjelasan:

Once upon a time, in a small village surrounded by lush green fields, lived a young girl named Lily. She was known for her adventurous spirit and love for nature. ←(Simple sentence)

Every day, she would explore the nearby forest, discovering hidden treasures and observing various creatures.

One sunny morning, as Lily was wandering through the woods, she stumbled upon a mysterious old book. Intrigued, she opened it and found a map leading to a hidden waterfall deep within the forest. Determined to find this enchanted waterfall, she embarked on a thrilling quest.

Armed with the map and her curiosity, Lily ventured into the heart of the forest. She walked for hours, admiring the towering trees and listening to the melodic chirping of birds. Suddenly, she heard a rustling sound behind her. She turned around and saw a fox staring at her with its bright, intelligent eyes.← (compound sentence)

With a smile on her face, Lily approached the fox cautiously. ←(Complex Sentence) They exchanged a few simple sentences, and the fox agreed to guide her to the magical waterfall. They walked side by side, their footsteps creating a rhythmic beat on the forest floor.

After a while, they reached a clearing where a breathtaking waterfall cascaded down into a crystal-clear pool. Lily was in awe of the beauty before her eyes. She thanked the fox for its help and spent the rest of the day enjoying the peacefulness of the place.

As the sun started to set, Lily realized that it was time to return home. She bid farewell to the waterfall, knowing that she would cherish this memory forever. With a heart full of gratitude, she made her way back through the forest, guided by the fading light.

In this story, simple sentences were used to describe Lily's daily activities and her encounters with nature. Compound sentences were utilized when Lily met the fox and when she reached the waterfall. Complex sentences were employed to illustrate Lily's feelings and thoughts during her journey. Lastly, a compound-complex sentence was used to convey the resolution of the story and the impact it had on Lily's life.

Pengertian Paragraf Complex Sentence

Dilansir dari EdPlace, complex sentence adalah kalimat yang terdiri dari satu independent clause atau kalimat tunggal dan satu atau lebih dependent clause. Dependent clause (klausa terikat) disebut juga fragment atau kalimat yang tidak lengkap dan tidak dapat berdiri sendiri.

24. materi complex sentence and compound sentence


Namanya juga sederhana (simple), jenis kalimat ini hanya terdiri dari 1 kalusa yang terdiri dari Subject dan Predicate. Meski hanya 1 klausa, kalimat ini sudah ememiliki arti yang utuh. Makanya 1 klause itu disebut Independet (berdiri sendiri), alias tidak butuh kalusa yang lain agar maksud dan artinya bias dipahami.
Contoh:
1. Some people like to go to market.

Beberapa orang suka pergi ke pasar.

2. Tono and Tini play a game together.

Tono dan Tini bermain game bersama.

3. Budi goes to the library and borrows some book.

Budi pergi ke perpustakaan dan meminjam beberapa bukuKalimat Simple, compound, dan complex sentence adalah 3 macam kalimat bahasa Inggris yang harus dipelajari terutama bagi yang ingin lebih memperdalam dan mengerti contoh discussion text, argumentative dan jenis teks lainnya. Pengertian sekaligus contoh kalimat simple, compund , dan complex sentence menduduki porsi terbesar dalam mendapat perhatian ketika ingin meningkatkan skill reading comprehension dan writing.

Macam-macam kalimat di bahasa Inggris:
1. Simple sentence ( 1 subject + 1 verb ) Objects or adverbs are optional
    I run a printing business. (S + V + O)
2. Compound sentence (compound subjects or compound verbs)
    I'm answering phone and typing emails at the same time (double verbs)
3. Complex sentence (1 dependent clause + 1 independent clause)
    I left my laptop at the office because my boss wanted to check my
    work.         
    (conjunction = because)
4. Compound Complex sentence (complex sentence with compound verbs or nouns)
    I left my laptop and folders because my boss and his assistant wanted to check my work.

35. apa perbedaan simple sentence, compound sentence, complex sentence, compound complex sentence?


Punctuation (Tanda Baca) pada Compound-Complex SentencePada dasarnya, compound-complex sentence mengikuti aturan penggunaan tanda baca yang berlaku terhadap compound dan complex sentence. Secara ringkas aturannya adalah sebagai berikut.Compound SentenceComplex Sentence
compound
Jika menggunakan coordinate conjunction (for, and, nor, but, or, yet, so), tanda baca koma digunakan tepat sebelum kata hubung tersebut.Jika menggunakan conjunctive adverb (furthermore, however, otherwise, when, etc), tanda baca semicolon (titik koma) digunakan tepat sebelum kata hubung dan koma tepat setelah kata hubung.Dapat menggunakan semicolon untuk menghubungkan dua independent clause yang sangat berhubungan.
complex sentencePada adverbial clause, jika berada di awal kalimat, diujung klausa ini diberi tanda baca koma.Pada adjective clause, jika menerangkan subject (berada di tengah kalimat), dapat diapit koma atau tidak tergantung dari penting atau tidak informasi yang dibawanya.
