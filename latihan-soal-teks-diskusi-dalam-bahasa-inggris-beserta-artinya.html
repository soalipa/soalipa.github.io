1. Reading Comprehension (Pemahaman Bacaan):

Teks:

"Read the following passage and answer the questions below."

Artinya:

"Bacalah teks berikut dan jawablah pertanyaan-pertanyaan di bawah ini."

Teks:

"Fill in the blanks with the appropriate words."

Artinya:

"Isilah titik-titik di bawah ini dengan kata-kata yang tepat."

3. True/False Questions (Pertanyaan Benar/Salah):

Teks:

"Decide whether the following statements are true or false."

Artinya:

"Tentukan apakah pernyataan-pernyataan berikut benar atau salah."

4. Multiple Choice Questions (Pertanyaan Pilihan Ganda):

Teks:

"Choose the best option that completes each sentence."

Artinya:

"Pilihlah opsi terbaik yang melengkapi setiap kalimat."

5. Matching Questions (Pertanyaan Pencocokan):

Teks:

"Match the items in List A with the items in List B."

Artinya:

"Cocokkan item-item dalam Daftar A dengan item-item dalam Daftar B."

How to Insert Sim Card Cellphone”

Cellphone is a modern communication device which connects one to the others by voice, written message and data. However this device can not work until the SIM card is inserted.

When inserting the SIM Card to cellphone, make sure that the cellphone has been switched off and follow the direction bellow :

First of all, press the locking catch and slide the cover then lift it off the phone.

After that, push two catches in the opposite directions and remove the battery.

Next, slide the SIM card carefully into the slot and make sure that the golden connect arson are facing to the connector of the phone.

Then, put the battery and align it until snaps into its place.

Finally, insert the two catches of the back cover corresponding slot in the phone and slide the cover forward button of the phone until locks into place

Don’t forget to switch on the cellphone. Wait until it is ready to use.

Ponsel adalah perangkat komunikasi modern yang menghubungkan orang satu dengan yang lain melalui suara, pesan tertulis dan data. Namun perangkat ini tidak bisa bekerja sampai kartu SIM dimasukkan.

12. contoh teks rencana liburan dalam bahasa inggris beserta artinya


Kelas : X
Pelajaran : bahasa Inggris
Kategori : plans
Kata kunci : simple future tense

Pembahasan:
Untuk menulis karangan tentang rencana, tense yang digunakan adalah simple future tense (will + v1 atau to be + going to + v1). Penggunaan will untuk menyatakan aktivitas yang akan dilakukan tanpa terencana terlebih dahulu. Sedangkan to be + going to + v1 untuk menyatakan aktivitas yang akan dilakukan dengan membuat rencana terlebih dahulu.

Contoh karangan tentang rencana liburan:
My mother and I are going to go Semarang next holiday. But, we are going to stay a night at Bandungan. We like to enjoy fresh water in the morning at there.

Ibu saya dan saya akan pergi ke Semarang liburan mendatang. Tetapi, kami akan tinggal semalam di Bandungan. Kamu suka menikmati udara segar di pagi hari di sana.

We are going to go to Bandungan by bus. My mother and I are backpackers. We like to go by economy transportation. From Jombor terminal bus, we are going to ride by economy bus to Ambarawa. And then, we are going to ride elf to Bandungan.

Kami akan pergi ke Bandungan dengan bis. Ibu saya dan saya adalah pejalan dengan tas punggung. Kami suka bepergian dengan transportasi ekonomi. Dari terminal bis Jombor, kami akan naik bis ekonomi ke Ambarawa. Dan kemudian, kami naik elf ke Bandungan.

Next day, we are going to go to Semarang by small bus. At Semarang, I am going to meet my senior collagues at there. And at noon, we will be back to Jogjakarta.

Hari berikutnya, kami akan pergi ke Semarang dengan bis kecil. Saya akan bertemu kakak senior di sana. Pada petang hari, kami akan kembali ke Jogjakarta.

35. Buatin report teks tentang corona dalam bahasa inggris beserta artinya


Jawaban:

December 2019, the world was shocked with an outbreak of a respiratory virus in the city of Wuhan, the capital city of Hubei province of the republic of China, recently known as the novel coronavirus or COVID-19. This virus belongs to a group of coronavirus that can infect human. The virus spread so rapidly that it has become a worldwide pandemic within only two months.

Coronavirus is a group of related viruses that cause diseases in mammals and bird. in humans, the corona virus causes mild respiratory infections, such as some flu cases, and others that can be deadly such as SARS, MERS, and COVID -19.

Symptoms in other species vary: in chickens, they cause upper respiratory tract disease, whereas in cows and pigs they cause diarrhea. There is no vaccine or antiviral drug to prevent or treat human coronavirus infections.

Coronaviruses are enveloped viruses with a single-stranded RNA genome and symmetrical helical nucleocapsids. The coronavirus genome number ranges from 27-34 kilos of base pair, the largest among known RNA viruses. The name coronavirus comes from the Latin corona which means crown, which refers to the appearance of virus particles (virions): they have edges that are reminiscent of the crown or corona of the sun.
